📝 Overview

The latter half of Usada Pekora's project to diagnose her horror game resistance. It starts from the continuation of the previous 'fear of hiding' test, where she desperately flees from a creature chasing her in the dark. In the subsequent 'fear of fleeing' test, she collects donuts while being pursued by an enemy, screaming at repeated jump scares but still fighting hard. After completing all tests, when the diagnosis results are announced, Pekora is surprised to see that she scored high in 'fear of surprise,' which she thought she was bad at. However, she complains about the unfairness of the system in the 'fear of hiding' and 'fear of fleeing' tests, appearing dissatisfied with the results. Finally, observations from the game regarding the accuracy of the tests are presented, further deepening the discussion about Pekora's horror game resistance. Throughout the stream, Pekora's comical reactions and lively interactions with viewers consistently created a cheerful atmosphere.
