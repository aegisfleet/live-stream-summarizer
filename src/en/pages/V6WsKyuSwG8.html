📝 Overview

A stream of the Nintendo Switch 2 version of 'Hogwarts Legacy' played by Shishiro Botan. School life fully began, completing Charms class where she learned the Levitation Charm (Levioso), and Defense Against the Dark Arts class involving duel training with Sebastian Sallow. Continuing the application of Charms, she challenged the mini-game "Summoner's Court," which uses the Summoning Charm (Accio), under Professor Ronen's guidance. She competed against her classmate Natsai Onai but was defeated. After the match, she learned that Natsai is a transfer student from Uagadou School of Magic and about the cultural background of non-wand magic. Finally, Professor Ronen assigned a new task, concluding the stream.

🎨 Mood: An atmosphere filled with excitement for the new school life at the wizarding school and the thrill of continuous combat, puzzles, and mini-games.
