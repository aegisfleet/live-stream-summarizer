📝 Overview

This is a streamed project featuring Hololive's La+ Darknesss, who, along with her subordinate Ishida, creates easy yet bizarre dishes called 'Genkai Meshi' (Extreme Meals). Three dishes are introduced: 'Traffic Light' (bell peppers and kimchi with mayonnaise), 'Warm & Fuzzy Set' (oatmeal and soup base microwaved), and 'Fruit Mix Ice' (frozen fruit with honey). The recipes, which disregard health and effort, and Ishida's realistic and comical reactions to tasting them, draw laughter from viewers.
