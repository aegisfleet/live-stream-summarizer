Site Description

[Important] This service is an experimental initiative using generative AI. The video summaries are created by AI and may contain inaccuracies. Please be aware of this.

By using this service, you are deemed to have agreed that the generative AI does not always output accurate information.


This service aims to help you quickly grasp the overview and highlights of the numerous Hololive stream archives, making it easier to find streams that interest you.

The technical mechanism is as follows:

  • Live stream schedule information is automatically fetched periodically from the Hololive Schedule.
  • Based on the fetched schedule, information such as video titles and URLs is collected from YouTube. *Since the generative AI is not limitless, only videos within 2 hours and 30 minutes are targeted.*
  • The content (audio/video) of the target video is sent directly to Google's Gemini API for analysis.
  • The Gemini API analyzes the video content and generates a summary text.
  • The generated summary is saved to the service and displayed for everyone to view.

This entire process is automated, aiming to make video content more accessible through the latest AI technology.

For more details, please see this article (Japanese).

If you find any errors in the content, please contact us via the "Correction Request" button.

Data Retention Period: Summarized data is automatically deleted after two weeks.


Disclaimer: The site author is not responsible for any errors in the information provided by the generative AI. Users should be aware that the information provided may be inaccurate and use this service at their own judgment and risk.


Common Summary Mistakes:

  • Misidentifying performers (e.g., listing someone who did not actually appear).
  • Inaccurate highlight times (timestamps may differ from the actual time).
  • Inaccurate stream content details (game progress or conversation content may differ from reality).
