📝 Overview

This is a stream where Ichijou Ririka hosts and challenges the cooperative climbing game 'PEAK' with Omaru Polka, Shirakami Fubuki, and Juufu Tei (Kanade). In this final part, facing a series of difficult routes and unpredictable happenings, everyone repeatedly falls, leading to a chaotic situation. Unusual incidents also occur, such as Kanade's character growing giant or a lollipop glitching. Despite suffering from low stamina, they all encourage each other and finally reach the summit of the high mountain (Day 6), which is the final checkpoint. It's the lively conclusion of a collaborative playthrough, sharing their success and expressing excitement for what's next.
