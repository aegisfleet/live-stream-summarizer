📝 Overview

This is the latter half of a watch-along stream for "Anison Fireworks - Anime Classics-", for which Inugami Korone serves as an ambassador. Inugami Korone was extremely excited throughout the entire stream, watching fireworks synchronized with the opening and ending animations of legendary anime works such as "Speed Racer", "Mazinger Z", "Cutie Honey", "Steel Jeeg", "Devilman", and "UFO Robot Grendizer". She cheered loudly, especially for the fireworks depicting Mazinger Z's combination sequence and Cutie Honey's transformation scene. The stream also covered the event stage hosted by MC BANBANBAN and the moving fireworks finale. The event's merchandise was introduced, and an additional performance in Kitakyushu on October 4th was announced. The stream concluded with the excitement still lingering.
