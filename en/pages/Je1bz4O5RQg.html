📝 Overview

A Hololive DEV_IS Among Us collaboration stream by Suimiya Kuwa. They played with various roles and the new map 'The Fungle'. Co-stars included Sakura Miko, Momosuzu Nene, Natsuiro Matsuri, Todoroki Hajime, Hatsuumi Uyu, Zankyo Minamo, Isaki Riona, Azumaya Kanade, and Zeze. From the middle to the end of the video, the crewmate team struggled to identify the impostors, repeatedly made wrong accusations, leading to suspicion and chaos. Suimiya Kuwa, in particular, fought hard to protect her teammates as a Detective and Guardian Angel, but struggled with tasks and movement due to unfamiliarity with the new map 'The Fungle'. Votes were skipped multiple times, and ultimately, impostors won one after another, depicting the crewmate team being cornered into a desperate situation.

🎨 Mood: Throughout, suspicion and chaos persisted, creating a sense of despair as the crewmate team was cornered. However, Suimiya Kuwa's persistent efforts to complete tasks and try to turn the situation around were impressive.
