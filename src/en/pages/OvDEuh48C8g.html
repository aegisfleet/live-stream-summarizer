📝 Overview

A post-stream following Shirakami Fubuki's birthday live "Meikai Halloween Night!". She expressed gratitude for the live's success, detailed the backstories of the songs chosen for the setlist, and explained the character settings of each guest (Omaru Polka, Sakura Miko, Amane Kanata, Yozora Mel, Kazama Iroha, Sakamata Chloe, Todoroki Hajime, Hiiro Moco). She particularly delved into the character design of Sukonbu, the residents of the Underworld (Meikai) which was the live's theme, Naira, the Queen of the Underworld who appeared as a guest, and the deep settings of the original song. She also announced the start of an irregular serialized novel, "FBKINGDOM STORY," to deepen the live's world view, while looking back at the concert and responding to fan comments.
