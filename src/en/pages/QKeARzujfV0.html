In this stream, Hajime Hoshi of Hololive ReGLOSS shared her memories of returning home to Hokkaido and the views on life and death she gained from that experience. She recounted episodes of her first-class experience, saying goodbye to her grandmother, and daring to eat alone at the popular ramen restaurant "Yamaokaya." Her story of struggling to find a late-night parfait in Susukino highlights her passion for food and adventurous spirit. She also passionately discussed the behind-the-scenes of her new song "Zisorara" and her desire for vocal improvement, inspired by watching "THE FIRST TAKE," conveying her sincere attitude of constantly seeking growth. Towards the end of the stream, she engaged in deep interactions with viewers, discussing foreign dramas and sharing her 고민 (concerns) about the next stream's content, creating many touching and relatable moments within a calm atmosphere.
