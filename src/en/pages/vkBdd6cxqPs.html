📝 Overview

A stream where Sakura Miko and Oozora Subaru team up to challenge the bomb defusal game 'Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes'. They aim to dismantle bombs by reading the manual while receiving instructions from viewers. The stream features thrilling and suspenseful moments, such as succeeding in the tutorial initially but then failing a Simon game and causing an explosion. They struggled with modules of varying difficulties like 'Double Money', 'A Big Step', 'Sequence Wires', and '8 Minutes', repeatedly failing due to timeouts or incorrect defusals. Especially during the '8 Minutes' stage, the stream depicted Sakura Miko's tears as she was overwhelmed by complex rules and fell into a panic, along with Oozora Subaru's struggle to keep giving instructions without giving up. Although they persevered until the very end, they ultimately couldn't clear it, concluding the stream with a sense of regret.
