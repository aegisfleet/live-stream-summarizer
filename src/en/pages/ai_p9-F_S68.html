📝 Overview

A collaboration stream featuring Shiranui Flare, Aki Rosenthal, Yuzuki Choco, and Amane Kanata playing the cooperative horror game 'R.E.P.O.'. They challenged treasure hunts across various stages, including spooky buildings, a museum, and an academy, encountering unique enemies like cyclopes, chefs, and giant eyes. Despite facing numerous game overs, they steadily progressed by trial and error, distributing health recovery items, purchasing enhancement items, and devising cooperative tactics to defeat enemies. The stream was filled with constant screams due to the many mishaps, but also abundant laughter among the members, making it lively and enjoyable. Towards the end, Flare fought valiantly alone, and Kanata attempted to clear a mission but ran out of time, indicating continued struggles. However, they ultimately all managed to escape and advance to the next stage. The stream concluded with a discussion about saving methods for the next play session, a reflection on the fun of playing with beginner members, and expressions of anticipation for their next game.
