📝 Overview

Ririka Ichijo and Kanade Otogise collaborated as 'Ririnade' to take on a unique project using ChatGPT. The project involves having ChatGPT diagnose their personalities and then recommend movies based on those diagnostic results. The entire session unfolds in a warm atmosphere, showcasing their individual and often surprising answers, unexpected diagnostic results from ChatGPT, and their reactions to the recommended movies. This collaborative stream also provides an opportunity for them to learn more about each other's personalities and preferences, making it enjoyable for viewers to participate.
