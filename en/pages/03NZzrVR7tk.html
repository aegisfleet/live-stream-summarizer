📝 Overview

The second half of the GeoGuessr project where AZKi and Riona Hibikizaki toured Kochi Prefecture's tourist spots. Riona's strong local pride and memory continued to shine, instantly identifying "Sakawa Naumann Karst," a place of fond memories from her hometown, and "Taizan Park," which she frequently visited as a child, scoring perfect points repeatedly. As a result, she achieved a high score of 24,973 points, proving her skill despite being a GeoGuessr beginner. Deep topics unique to Kochi, such as the difference between Tosa-ben and Hata-ben dialects and local drinking culture, also emerged, resulting in a broadcast that conveyed the charm of Kochi in a consistently friendly atmosphere.
