📝 Overview

The Hololive Koshien 2025 Draft Conference was held, with four managers—Sakura Miko, Houshou Marine, Hakui Koyori, and Ichijou Ririka—participating. Each expressed their determination to lead their team to victory, carrying the regret from last year. After the draft rules were explained, players were selected via a bidding system for rounds 1-4 and a roulette system from round 5 onwards. Finally, the draft members and regions for each high school were decided. Following the draft conference, the schedule for upcoming training streams and detailed training rules were announced, with each manager reiterating their enthusiasm for victory and calling for warm support from their listeners.

🎨 Mood: In a lively and enthusiastic atmosphere, each manager vowed to avenge last year's defeat, showing strong determination for this year's championship, and raising anticipation for future training streams.
