📝 Overview

A collaborative stream featuring members of Hololive Advent and Hololive Justice from Hololive EN in a team chess match. All participants were chess beginners, and professional chess players were invited as coaches. The stream progressed in a friendly atmosphere, starting with an explanation of basic chess rules. After resetting the previous game, a new match began again with an E4 opening. With advice from the coaches, each member contributed ideas, applying strategies for piece development, center control, attack, and defense from the opening to the middlegame. White continued to attack aggressively and ultimately won with a checkmate. After the game, members discussed the match, and an announcement for the Hololive Indonesia concert was made.
