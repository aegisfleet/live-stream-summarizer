📝 Overview

Usada Pekora from Hololive conducted the final chapter of her Moon-Viewing Burger comparison stream. She raved about McDonald's "Melty Tsukimi Sukiyaki" burger, and then, for her first attempt at Komeda Coffee's "Otsukimi Full Moon Burger," she couldn't hide her surprise and emotion at its overwhelming size and elegant taste. Finally, she savored Kentucky's "Mochi Custard Tsukimi Pie." Pekora, having compared all the Tsukimi products, concluded that this year's Tsukimi Burger project was of the highest level, bringing the stream to a successful close.
