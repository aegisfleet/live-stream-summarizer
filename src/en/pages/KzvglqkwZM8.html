📝 Overview

Ichijo Ririka of hololive DEV_IS plays the horror game 'Japan Property Anomaly Monitoring Association' with fellow member Kanade. Starting with an introduction to the game's content, which is based on real-life haunted properties, and disturbing rumors surrounding its development, fear is already heightened from the stage of reading out the terms of use and warning messages. When monitoring work begins, Ichijo Ririka reports anomalies in the Akita Prefecture property and earns a reward. In the subsequent Nara Prefecture property, Kanade actively discovers numerous anomalies. However, during a re-attempt to monitor N Inn in Gifu Prefecture, they miss many anomalies, resulting in no reward again. It is revealed that the game's progression condition is to report all anomalies, and a re-attempt is decided for the next stream.

🎨 Mood: Amidst rising fear and tension, the streamers' unique reactions and their dynamic interactions with Kanade entertain the viewers. Especially towards the end, despite a mix of frustration and resignation, they show eagerness for the next stream.
