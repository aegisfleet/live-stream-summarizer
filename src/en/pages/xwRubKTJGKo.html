📝 Overview

This video records the experiences at Tenseien in Hakone Yumoto, a relaxing spot, as the first collaborative research project between hololive's Hakui Koyori and D-LAB's "Voice Play Project." The goal was to relax the usually busy Koyori and collect voice samples. After correctly answering a quiz about her own voice, she enjoyed a luxurious room with an open-air bath, collecting voice data while physically and mentally relaxed through foot baths and delicious meals.

🎨 Mood: Experimental, yet consistently calm and relaxed atmosphere
