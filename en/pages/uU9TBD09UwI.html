📝 Overview

This is a stream where Shirakami Fubuki of Hololive re-challenges the horror adventure game '66 Confessions of Sunpinel Castle'. Starting from '28 Confessions,' a continuation from the previous stream, this time she plays up to the middle of '00 Confessions.' The game's rule is to find the anomalous being lurking among the seven people visiting the old castle: if a strange phenomenon occurs, shoot them with a gun; otherwise, ring the bell. Fubuki-san focused on finding subtle anomalies hidden in familiar scenery, and her comical reactions upon recognizing these changes remain a highlight.
