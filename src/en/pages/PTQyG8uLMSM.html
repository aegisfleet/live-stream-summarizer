📝 Overview

This is a stream where Otonose Kanade plays the viewer-participation digging game "Keep Digging". She cooperates with viewers to dig deep underground, discovering minerals and valuable items. After explaining the game and recruiting participants in the early stages, Kanade is surprised by the viewers' incredible digging speed. Together, they mine deep-seated minerals to earn money. Upgrading their equipment repeatedly, they explore ruins resembling pyramids and caves where giant gems lie dormant, reaching a secret facility more than 900m deep. After mining in a magma zone, pressing the final button causes the city to explode, successfully clearing the game. The atmosphere is cheerful and lively, enjoying the game with viewers, and getting excited with each item discovery.

🎨 Mood: A consistently friendly atmosphere with the surprise and sense of achievement from cooperative play.
