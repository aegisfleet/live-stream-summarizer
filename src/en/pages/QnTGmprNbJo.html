📝 Overview

The managers of Hololive Koshien 2025—Sakura Miko, Houshou Marine, Ichijou Ririka—along with host Hakui Koyori, gathered to take on "Minna de BBQ (Baseball Quiz)," a quiz challenging their baseball knowledge. In the latter half of the stream, advanced rule and status questions, particularly those related to Success Nine (Eikan Nine), were asked, and the managers struggled, utilizing their in-game knowledge. The quiz covered topics like the rule for a ball bouncing into the stands, 'Chance'/'Pinch' situations, batter abilities and preferred hitting zones, and culminated in a lively question about Manager Koyori's favorite 'Blue Trait' (Aotoku). Ichijou Ririka ultimately won the competition. The stream was constantly lively and full of laughter, raising anticipation for Holo Koshien.
