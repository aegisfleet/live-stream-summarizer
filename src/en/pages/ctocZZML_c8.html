📝 Overview

Omaru Polka streams playing 'Goblin Gnome Horn' on Hololive. The game involves a goblin climbing the ranks through various mini-game-style part-time jobs. Polka achieved S-ranks early on in the power generation and dandelion placement mini-games. She then struggled with the high difficulty of 'Patrol' but achieved an S-rank after persistent attempts. Ultimately, she obtained S-ranks in all mini-games and cleared the game with the 'Bloodless Surrender End.' Afterwards, she attempted the battle end but repeatedly lost against powerful enemies, choosing the peaceful ending to conclude the stream. Polka's emotional enjoyment of the game made for a memorable stream.

🎨 Mood: Throughout the stream, Polka's intense focus on the game and enthusiasm for achieving S-ranks in mini-games were palpable. Especially when she earned an S-rank in a high-difficulty game, she erupted with great joy and a sense of accomplishment. Despite expressing frustration and regret over the difficulty, her dedicated attitude of never giving up and continuously challenging herself was striking.
