📝 Overview

This is a stream where Momosuzu Nene plays the game 'Get To Work', a game about helping a bald old man get promoted. The protagonist is a bald old man on rollerblades. Nene struggles with the unfamiliar controls but aims for the goal by rolling the bald old man. The objective is to skillfully control the physics-driven character to reach the interview. After failing the interview and restarting as a warehouse intern, Nene gradually masters the controls, despite struggling with wall climbing, complex routes, and high-altitude movements. She continues to challenge herself, undeterred by numerous failures and taunting messages from the game, and eventually succeeds in completing the sorting center staff training. Nene's unfamiliar controls, the bald old man's comical movements, and their journey of growth deliver laughter and encouragement to viewers.
