📝 Overview

Joufutei Raden and Kitashirakawa Kakapo, celebrating their 2nd debut anniversary, embark on a special trip to explore the charm and science of Bizen ware. Under the guidance of Mr. Takahiro Hosokawa, they experience pottery making and delve into the scientific elucidation of Bizen ware at Okayama University of Science. The trip concludes with relaxation at a hot spring inn overlooking the Seto Ohashi Bridge, reflecting on the 18-hour shoot. Through heartwarming interactions with Bizen ware craftsman Mr. Sugata, they are once again deeply moved by the profoundness of traditional crafts and the kindness of people. They look forward to their next journey exploring traditional crafts, concluding a fulfilling Bizen chapter.
