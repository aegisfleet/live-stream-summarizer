Overview

Moona from Hololive Indonesia 1st Generation streamed the multiplayer mode of the farm simulation game "Coral Island". Together with 3 guests selected from the viewers, she started developing a new farm named "Boeland". The stream began with an apology for speaker echo at the start, followed by busy farm work such as clearing overgrown land and repairing the farmhouse. A highlight was the frequent mishaps where players successively collapsed from exhaustion, with Moona herself eventually collapsing. Even after recovering at the hospital, they returned to the farm, cooperatively progressing through the game by clearing land, tending crops, and shopping at stores. Moona went into town to interact with various NPCs, and despite encountering multiplayer bugs, steadily developed the farm. The stream depicted Moona and her friends struggling with tough yet lively and harmonious farm work, concluding with a thank you to viewers and an announcement for the next stream.

Mood: Harmonious and cooperative, yet sometimes exhausting
