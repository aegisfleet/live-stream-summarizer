📝 Overview

Hololive's Aki Rosenthal started a personal stream after her Mario Kart World experience on Abema's program 'Holocoe'. She began playing with the resolve of 'I won't be called a noob anymore,' aiming to unlock new courses. She narrowly lost to Catherine, placing 2nd in 'Dino Dino Jungle' in the Banana Cup, 3rd in 'Mystery Temple,' and secured 1st place in four other courses. During this time, she unlocked three new characters: Catherine, King Boo, and Bowser Jr. She achieved 1st place in 'Rainbow Road' in the Special Cup and 'Shupopo Coaster' in the Mushroom Cup, then continued racing in 'DK Space Center' and 'Moo Moo Meadows'. Although there were moments where she dropped in rank due to item attacks and falling off the course, her persistence in catching up was impressive. The stream maintained a bright and enjoyable atmosphere throughout, and she mentioned planning to play survival mode next time.
