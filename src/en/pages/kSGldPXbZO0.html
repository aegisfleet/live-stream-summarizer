📝 Overview

This is a project where Omaru Polka, a 5th generation Hololive member, buys convenience store items for under 1000 yen to significantly boost her happiness. Together with program staff member Izutsu, she lists items she wants to eat without worrying about the price. Ultimately, she chose 5 items: "melon pan," "vanilla yogurt," "Red Bull Sugarfree," "miso ramen," and "konbu onigiri," totaling 1070 yen, which slightly exceeded the budget, but Polka's happiness soared.

🎨 Mood: The atmosphere was consistently cheerful, conveying how her happiness increased as the project progressed.
