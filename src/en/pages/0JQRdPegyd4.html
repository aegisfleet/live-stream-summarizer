📝 Overview

Hakui Koyori's weekly morning stream 'AsaKoyo!' reached its memorable 250th broadcast. She spoke of her joy and gratitude for continuing the stream every week without a break since her debut, and congratulatory messages from viewers were also introduced. Congratulatory messages also arrived from Tokoyami Towa and Ichijou Ririka, with Koyori particularly confused by Ririka's mysterious 'Konbu!' remark. In 'Koyori's Hot News,' which delivers the latest information on Hololive as a whole, ReGLOSS's 1st live concert, official merchandise, magazine features, store and game collaborations, and original song MVs were introduced. The broadcast also hyped up the announcement of Koyori-produced 'Hololive Acoustic Live' and the new short video series 'MofuKoyo.' In the latter half's 'Katsu! Appare Ikkatsu!' segment, interesting episodes from other members were successively introduced, such as the truth behind the 'crossing the line...?' commotion at the Hololive Fireworks Festival, Tokoyami Towa's incident of directly 'punishing' a scalper, Oozora Subaru's 'eccentric behavior' episode before her solo live concert, and Roboco-san's story of almost making sake stew by mistaking sake for milk. To conclude, a tarot reading announced 'Mozart' as the lucky item, which also confused viewers. Filled with various new information and entertaining episodes, this commemorative broadcast is a must-watch for fans.

🎨 Mood: A consistently warm atmosphere filled with gratitude and joy.
