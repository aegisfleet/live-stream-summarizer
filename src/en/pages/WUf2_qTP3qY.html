📝 Overview

Shishiro Botan and her team members streamed a joint practice session for the Tokyo Game Show "Trigger of Fate" event. They confirmed the basic game rules, character/weapon skills, and item performance, then explored team coordination tactics in a practical format. They experienced unique elements such as automatic healing item equipping, flying cars, and item enhancement, culminating in their first victory after an intense battle. In the latter half of the practice, they engaged in offensive and defensive battles, conscious of team tactics, using vehicles to escape the anti-zone while defeating enemies. They ultimately secured another victory. At the end of the stream, they finalized weapon adjustments in the training ground, concluding the practice. They thanked their viewers and announced their streaming schedule for the next day, as well as the premiere of an early access gameplay video for another game.

🎨 Mood: A consistently cheerful atmosphere, with occasional laughter, as they seriously engaged in the game and shared the joy of victory.
