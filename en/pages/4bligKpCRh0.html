📝 Overview

Hololive's Shiranui Flare, Shirakami Fubuki, Omaru Polka, and Tsunomaki Watame embark on a journey to the summit in the cooperative climbing game 'PEAK'. They overcome harsh environments such as poisonous plants, wild beasts, and scorching desert areas through items, wisdom, and strong teamwork. Despite encountering mishaps where members drop out, they unite towards the shared goal of eating onigiri at the summit and continue their challenge. This time, the adventure in the desert area is particularly highlighted, showing them struggling with the heat that changes with the time of day and unique mechanics like cacti, yet cooperating to forge a path forward. In the final chapter, they enter a dangerous area with erupting magma. Under extreme conditions, Fubuki attempts a perilous ascent but runs out of strength, leading to a game over. Afterward, the members enjoy trying on new hats at their base and discuss the rules for the new 'Card Battle' mode for the next stream, building anticipation in a peaceful atmosphere.

🎨 Mood: Consistently lively, sometimes frantic due to mishaps, but filled with teamwork and laughter through cooperative play.
