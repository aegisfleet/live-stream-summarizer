Overview

This is a live stream of the cooperative survival game 'PEAK' featuring Akai Haato, Shiori Novella, and IRyS. After a plane crash, the players embark on a survival challenge on a deserted island, struggling with poisonous plants, dangerous terrain, and managing food and stamina. They repeatedly die and retry. Iris's unpredictable actions lead to comical mishaps as they explore diverse environments like jungles, volcanoes, snowy mountains, and deserts. Despite facing difficulties, they assist each other and sometimes accidentally attack each other, leading to unpredictable developments. In the final session, they successfully escaped from the airport, completing their journey safely.

Mood: Comical and full of mishaps throughout, yet depicting cooperation and perseverance in the face of difficulties.
