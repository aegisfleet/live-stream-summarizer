📝 Overview

This is a stream where Takane Lui provided her final prediction and live commentary for the Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe. After detailed analysis of each participating horse in the first half, Lui purchased her final betting tickets, centered on her favorite, Mini Hawk. Subsequently, Sakura Miko suddenly joined as a guest, and the excitement grew as they shared their predictions while watching the race. During the actual race, Chronotenol led early on, but in the final straight, Dariz, whom Sakura Miko had favored, won. Although Lui lost her bet, she rejoiced in Miko's huge victory, concluding the stream in great excitement.
