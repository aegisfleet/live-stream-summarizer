📝 Overview

Shirakami Fubuki and Omaru Polka conducted a special project commemorating the launch of the Halloween Jumbo Lottery. They explained that a lottery official saw their previous lottery stream, which led to this collaboration. In the video, they funnily learned about lottery history, types, revenue distribution, and social contributions through a quiz format. The project also included information on Halloween Jumbo campaigns and a simulation of how they would spend a large prize if they won. Referring to a special guidebook only given to high-prize winners, Shirakami Fubuki and Omaru Polka discussed their dreams and realistic plans for using the money, engaging in lively discussions with viewers.
