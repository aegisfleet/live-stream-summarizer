This is a stream releasing unaired footage from Akai Haato's visit to the cursed object room of cursed object collector Toshiyuki Tanaka. Furthermore, the dual nature of curses and charms, a secret technique that can result in both good and bad curses, and the horrifying history of a certain family related to the 'Nue's Arm' are revealed. At the end of the video, a warning is given about the possibility of souls residing in everyday objects and the caution required in handling them.
