📝 Overview

This is a project where Omaru Polka experiences the "totonou" (getting into a state of deep relaxation/being in sync, often after a sauna). Instead of a regular sauna, the goal is to regulate the autonomic nervous system by providing mental stimulation with the settings "encouragement = sauna" and "insults = cold bath." The video depicts Polka's confusion and anger due to the merciless insults and tactless encouragement from new staff member Izutsu (a scriptwriter), ultimately leaving Polka in an "exhausted" state. Staff Izutsu also stated that he didn't achieve "totonou," concluding the harsh project with "never do this even with close friends."
