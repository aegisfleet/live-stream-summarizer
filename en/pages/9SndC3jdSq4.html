📝 Overview

This is the introduction before Hololive's Roboco-san begins her gameplay stream of Harvest Moon: Grand Bazaar. On the waiting screen before the stream starts, a wide variety of announcements are presented in a slideshow format, including Roboco-san's membership benefits, Hololive 0th Generation anniversary merchandise, membership-exclusive wallpapers, past ASMRs, animated LINE stickers, the Hololive card game, official outfits, cover songs (Ten Ten Tengoku Jigoku, Oni no Utage), and the ASMR/voice drama "Starlord". After that, Roboco-san herself appears, mentions issues such as audio delay and comment display delay that occurred during the stream, and suggests restarting the stream, at which point the analysis scope ends.

🎨 Mood: Anticipation mixed with a somewhat flustered atmosphere due to streaming issues
