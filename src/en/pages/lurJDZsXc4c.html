📝 Overview

This is a stream where Tokino Sora plays Harvest Moon 64 for the first time. Starting with character and farm name settings, she experiences the basics of farm life, such as turnip cultivation, fishing, and interacting with residents. While completing various events, including meeting rival characters and interacting with the Sprites, she also deepens her understanding of the game system. In particular, she explains that Harvest Moon 64 has no clear ending, allowing players freedom in how time flows and how they set their goals, depending on their playstyle. She also shows her struggle to balance her playstyle of aiming for marriage with her 'oshi' (favorite character) and the need for efficient farming and economic activities. She talks about the game being a memorable part of her childhood, expresses her enthusiasm for future streams, thanks the viewers, and concludes the broadcast.
