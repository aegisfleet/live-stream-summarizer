📝 Overview

A solo speedrun project for the horror game "R.E.P.O.". Omaru Polka, Shiranui Flare, and Shirakami Fubuki competed to see who could advance furthest in the stages within a 2-hour time limit. Map and shop rerolls were allowed, but resets after death were not. From the beginning, they proceeded with item collection and equipment enhancement, but were repeatedly blocked by enemies, leading to multiple game overs and restarts from Stage 1. In the latter half, they obtained powerful weapons, but experienced another game over in Stage 4. Ultimately, Shirakami Fubuki reached Level 8, Shiranui Flare Level 6, and Omaru Polka Level 4, making Shirakami Fubuki the winner. Polka, while showing her frustration, also expressed enthusiasm for the next event.
