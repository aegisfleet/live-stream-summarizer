📝 Overview

This is a travelogue featuring Hololive's Tsunomaki Watame and Shigure Ui as they explore Shikoku (Kagawa and Ehime prefectures). In Kagawa, they challenge themselves with an udon-making experience at "Nakano Udon School" and taste the udon they made. Afterwards, they move to Ehime and compare various types of mandarin orange juice at a unique shop where mandarin orange juice comes out of a tap. They further enjoy sightseeing spots and local gourmet food in Matsuyama (such as Boned Chicken, Tai Meshi, Botchan Dango, etc.), depicting how they fully enjoy the charm of the region throughout their 2-night, 3-day trip.

🎨 Mood: A travel video with a consistently bright and peaceful atmosphere, where the two's honest reactions and interactions regarding food and experiences are enjoyable.
