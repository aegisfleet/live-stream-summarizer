📝 Overview

This project is a 3D offline collaboration where streamer Usada Pekora, along with guests Shirogane Noel, La+ Darknesss, and Juufu Ruruka, take on a revenge challenge of 'watermelon smashing with rubber bands'. Last time, they failed by dropping the watermelon, so this time they prepared a special stand and were fully ready. To boost their motivation for smashing the watermelon, they ate extremely spicy mochi, causing a big commotion from the start. From the middle to the end, they continuously applied rubber bands until the watermelon spectacularly exploded, successfully achieving their revenge. At the end of the video, they ate the exploded watermelon, and then Juufu Ruruka, one of the guests, announced the release of her group 'ReGLOSS's album and an upcoming live concert.

🎨 Mood: Some mishaps at the beginning, but overall a friendly and lively atmosphere.
