Overview

An emergency impromptu collaboration stream of Among Us featuring Sakura Miko from hololive. A stellar lineup of members including Shirakami Fubuki, Ookami Mio, Nekomata Okayu, Roboco, Hoshimachi Suisei, Tokoyami Towa, Omaru Polka, and Hakui Koyori participated, engaging in an unpredictable game of deception. Each round featured intense discussions and strategic actions, with the psychological warfare of the Imposters and the deductions of the Crewmates entertaining the viewers. Sakura Miko struggled as a Crewmate but was occasionally wrongly ejected. Shirakami Fubuki's skillful Imposter play particularly shined in this session.
