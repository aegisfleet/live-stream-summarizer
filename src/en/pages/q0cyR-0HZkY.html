📝 Overview

As the final day of Hololive's official channel's 3-night consecutive project 'Natsu Kyun,' Natsuiro Matsuri and Akai Haato (Haachama) produced and presented two 'kyun-kyun' (heart-thumping) romance movies to make viewers' hearts flutter. The first movie featured a scenario where they were studying for a retest at school during summer break, with Haato making a bold invitation to Matsuri. The second movie was set during a festival date, filled with heart-throbbing lines like inviting someone to see fireworks and complimenting their yukata. Overall, it was a lively and fun project, packed with cuteness and humor.
