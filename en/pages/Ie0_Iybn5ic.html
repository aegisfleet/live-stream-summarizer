📝 Overview

Tokino Sora's singing stream where she carefully selected her own original songs that she feels are 'emotional,' performing them while sharing the stories behind them and the feelings embedded in their lyrics. Even when it was difficult for her to produce sound, her emotionally rich singing was moving, creating a warm atmosphere that fostered a bond with her listeners. This time, focusing on 'Hanatoki no Sora' from her 5th-anniversary album 'hololive,' she looked back at 'emotional songs' from the early days of her career, singing with gratitude, and finally performed 'Furefure-LOVE' as an encore.

🎨 Mood: High vocal ability, many moving moments. An atmosphere filled with warm interaction with listeners.
