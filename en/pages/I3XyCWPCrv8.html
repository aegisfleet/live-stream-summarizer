Overview

This is the Ueno edition of 'Shishiron's Summer Vacation,' a special project commemorating Shishiro Botan's 5th anniversary. It features a realistic walk-through video utilizing Mokopi technology, showcasing Ueno's cityscape, historical spots, shopping streets, museums, and long-established restaurants. In the latter half of the video, 5th-anniversary merchandise such as the 'Fun Labor T-shirt,' 'Menya Botan Apron,' and 'Shishiron & SSRB Drawstring Bag Set' were introduced, along with the design of the 'Menya Botan Staff Recruitment Card' given as a bonus for purchasing the full set. Details and sales information for the merchandise were shared, and while reflecting on the current project, expectations for next year's 'Shishiron's Summer Vacation' were also discussed.

Mood: A consistently calm and peaceful atmosphere throughout, filled with discovery and nostalgia during the stroll, and a cheerful atmosphere during the merchandise introduction.
