📝 Overview

Hakui Koyori streamed a behind-the-scenes talk and review session for her 'Full Live 3D Acoustic Live in Summer'. She expressed gratitude to fans who watched the live and shared her deep emotion from reading their warm comments during the event. She discussed the casting process, including how AZKi, Oogami Mio, and Takane Lui came to participate after the initial JP member selection, the reason for Kaela Kovalskia's surprise appearance, and the charms of each member. She also detailed how the subtitle 'in Summer' influenced song selection, and the meticulous attention to detail in the production of the opening song 'Zankyosanka' and Kaela and Biboo's 'Kaze to Issho ni', expressing thanks to staff and performers. In the middle section, episodes related to the members' harmonies in 'Sparkle' and 'Secret Base', a technical issue and recovery during 'Garnet', and the production secrets and staging of 'Uchiage Hanabi' were shared. After conveying her gratitude for the overall success of the live, she discussed the potential for future acoustic lives, her own growth in singing ability, and her enthusiasm for upcoming activities.
