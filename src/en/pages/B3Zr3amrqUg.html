📝 Overview

VTuber Kogane Hisatora returned from hiatus and delivered a long-awaited singing stream. She powerfully sang a wide range of songs including "Seesaw Game," "Saigo no Ame," "Aoi Bench," "Mahou no Kotoba," and "Haru wa Yuku," while sharing her experience of overcoming a sore throat due to COVID-19 infection, memories with her parents, and her feelings during the hiatus. Towards the end, she got excited with viewers over a "Kamado Tanjiro no Uta" meme and concluded emotionally with Miki Imai's "PIECE OF MY WISH." She also made several announcements regarding her post-hiatus activities, such as upcoming "utaite" (cover song) videos and a special stream on FLOW GLLOW.
