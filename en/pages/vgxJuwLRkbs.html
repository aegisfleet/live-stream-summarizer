📝 Overview

This stream features Usada Pekora's popular segment where she conducts a surprise room check of her viewers. After overcoming a mishap at the beginning of the stream where her own room was accidentally revealed due to equipment trouble, the viewer-submitted room tours continued. A wide variety of rooms appeared one after another, including otaku rooms, Japanese-style rooms, rooms just after moving, and female viewers' rooms. Pekora sharply commented on the individuality of each room and the displayed merchandise (Hololive members, anime, games, etc.), sometimes delivering affectionate teasing. In particular, for a very luxurious and tidy room, she exclaimed, "It's a rich person's house!" while also affectionately retorting, "You shouldn't be watching this in such a lovely living room!" For rooms overflowing with a massive amount of merchandise, she playfully asked, "Can you really relax here?!", drawing laughter. The stream maintained a lively and enjoyable atmosphere throughout.
