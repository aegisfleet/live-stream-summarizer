📝 Overview

A stream by Hololive's Aki Rosenthal to boost her morale for the 'Mahjong Soul Underdog League' starting tomorrow. After touching on the league's overview and opponents, she used Mahjong Soul's game record analysis feature to analyze her own mahjong play. She aimed to improve her skills, experiencing highs and lows with the AI's harsh critiques. Furthermore, she meticulously analyzed other players' past games using the game record analysis, learning deep mahjong knowledge from tactical advice. She then drew a total of 120 gachas in Mahjong Soul, acquiring new mahjong player characters and decorations. Aki Rosenthal, feeling her own growth after achieving good results in a friendly match, once again expressed her enthusiasm for the upcoming league, concluding the stream by thanking viewers and asking for their support.

🎨 Mood: A consistently calm and friendly atmosphere, mixed with excitement for the mahjong league, the thrill of gacha, and seriousness towards the learning gained from game record analysis.
