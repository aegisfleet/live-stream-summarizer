📝 Overview

Natsuiro Matsuri's late-night ballad singing stream, aimed at putting viewers to sleep. In a moist and gentle atmosphere, she performed various genres of songs in ballad arrangements, including "Inori no Uta (Song of Prayer)," "Chiisana Koi no Uta (Little Love Song)," "Marigold," "Zankoku na Tenshi no Thesis (A Cruel Angel's Thesis)," "Country Road," and "secret base ~Kimi ga Kureta Mono~," as well as "Mou Sukoshi Dake (Just a Little More)," "Nemurenai Yoru wo Koete (Beyond Sleepless Nights)," "Hajimete no Chu (First Kiss)," "I love you, I love you, I love you, so much!," "Heroine," and "I love you (to much)." She also delivered a healing singing voice while responding to viewer requests.

🎨 Mood: A consistently calm and healing atmosphere. Her singing voice, sometimes mellow, sometimes poignant, resonated, stirring the hearts of viewers. Her emotional singing and gentle interaction with viewers were impressive.
