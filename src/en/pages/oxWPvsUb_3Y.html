Overview

AZKi, Yukihana Lamy, and Hakui Koyori play the horror game "Japan Stigmatized Property Monitoring Association," set in real-life stigmatized properties. After successfully completing the previous monitoring task at Akita Prefecture's House A and earning a reward, they take on the next property, Ibaraki Prefecture's House S. This high-difficulty mission requires careful attention to avoid missing even minor abnormal phenomena, as emergency notifications frequently appeared from the start. The players continued their monitoring, screaming but cooperating, reporting 20 abnormalities and missing 3, yet still completing the task successfully. They earned a high reward, and the stream was filled with a sense of accomplishment from overcoming their fear.

Mood: A horror experience filled with screams and shouts from beginning to end.
