📝 Overview

A gameplay stream of the horror game 'Nihon Jiko Bukken Kanshi Kyokai' (Japan Property Accident Surveillance Association). Suisei Hoshimachi can't hide her fear and surprise at the game content, which is based on actual property accident surveillance work. She monitors multiple "accident properties" and screams at strange anomalies captured on camera, yet sometimes encourages herself with bold statements. Her emotional reactions continue, especially her frustration every time she misses humanoid ghosts or hidden anomalies. In this session, she encounters disappearing objects and a male ghost, and also discusses the fictional nature of the game. In the final work report, she is surprised by the large number of missed items.

🎨 Mood: A mix of fear and bewilderment, occasionally with a confident atmosphere
