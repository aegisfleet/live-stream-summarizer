📝 Overview

VTuber Natsuiro Matsuri's ranked match stream for "Shadowverse: Worlds Beyond". In the beginning, she challenged ranked matches while mentioning daily missions and the Grand Prix final round. Using a Royal deck, she played a total of 3 matches against Runecraft, Royal, and Bishop players, winning all of them. In the first match, in particular, she tenaciously overcame a close situation, and in the third match, she broke through the opponent's solid defense and recovery, achieving an impressive 7-win streak. Afterward, she narrowly lost a Bishop match but then continued her winning streak in Royal mirror matches and Bloodcraft matches. In the latter half, she enjoyed collecting items and dressing up her avatar in the park, then re-challenged ranked matches. Towards the end, intense battles against Runecraft continued, with several close losses, but ultimately she secured victory with Albert's lethal blow, recording a total of 12 consecutive wins. Throughout the stream, her never-give-up attitude, even through ups and downs, and her trial-and-error approach, such as attempting to change game settings, were impressive.
