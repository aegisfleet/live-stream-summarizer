📝 Overview

Momosuzu Nene takes on 'Japan Property Monitoring Association' as a summer horror project. It's a game where she becomes a monitor with a duty to report supernatural phenomena, checking surveillance camera footage for anomalies. She navigates the eerie spaces of traditional Japanese houses, switching between cameras. Nene diligently performs her monitoring duties, despite being startled by occasional noises and static. In the mid-section, she tackles the night monitoring of a ryokan (Japanese inn). She manages to report all anomalies despite being surprised by various strange occurrences throughout the inn. In the final stage, she clears the A House monitoring duty in HARD mode. Overcoming fears like the screen going completely dark or jump scares with close-up faces, she successfully completes the horror project and lets out a cry of joy.

🎨 Mood: A somewhat tense atmosphere, but with added comicality due to the streamer's reactions.
