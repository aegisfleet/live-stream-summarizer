📝 Overview

A collaboration stream between Rinne Chihaya (Hololive DEV IS) and Bancho (FLOWGLOW). They teamed up as "Bunbun Budodo" to tackle cooperative play in the realistic parking simulation game, 'Parking Sim'. They attempted basic parking missions (like garage parking and parallel parking), challenging stages such as backing up on active train tracks, and unique high-difficulty levels set at festival grounds and water obstacle courses. Towards the end, they faced a stage covered in snow and ice. Bancho's skillful driving technique shone brightly as she deftly avoided snowballs and controlled the car on the slippery ice, successfully clearing the final stage. The stream was charming throughout, marked by the duo's lively banter.
