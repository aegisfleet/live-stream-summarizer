Overview

This stream is a birthday project for Ookami Mio, where she provides "omotenashi" (hospitality) to Hyakki Ayame and Takane Lui, whom she is usually indebted to. In the first half, Takane Lui is taken to Myogi Shrine, a power spot in Gunma, early in the morning, where she experiences prayer and waterfall meditation (Takigyo). Lui screamed at the coldness of the waterfall but also felt a sense of accomplishment, making it an emotional surprise. In the second half, Mio cooks special, heartfelt Mapo Tofu for Hyakki Ayame, charming the guest with her culinary skills. Furthermore, as a surprise from Hyakki Ayame, Mio's birthday cake, which she hadn't been able to eat for years, was finally allowed, and she burst with joy. The stream also featured the unveiling of a new song, the announcement of a 2nd album, and her first solo live concert, making it a packed event. The entire stream had a warm and friendly atmosphere, conveying a sense of gratitude.
