📝 Overview

A special broadcast commemorating the 1st anniversary of the Hololive Official Card Game (Holoca). Announcements included new starter decks, supplies, event information, and the introduction of a new rarity card, "Holomen Rare." Additionally, updates to the game's preparation rules, the decision to live stream tournaments on the official Holoca YouTube channel launching in October 2025, and a reading of a letter from the producer expressing gratitude and future operational policies were made. The broadcast celebrated the 1st anniversary, vowing further development of Holoca and the pursuit of "the fun of playing" and "the fun of watching," proceeding in a consistently amicable atmosphere.
