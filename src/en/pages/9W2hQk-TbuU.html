📝 Overview

A collaborative GeoGuessr stream featuring AZKi and Natsuiro Matsuri. AZKi created an original map with 30 tourist spots in Aichi Prefecture beforehand, and together with Natsuiro Matsuri, they explore the charms of Aichi. They visit unique Aichi sightseeing spots and local gourmet locations such as Nagoya Castle, Fujitaya Ooanmaki, Meiji-mura Museum, Sugakiya, Moricoro Park, and Kinshachi Yokocho. With Matsuri-chan's local knowledge and nostalgic stories, the stream maintains a consistently harmonious atmosphere while introducing Aichi's appeal. Even in difficult guessing locations, the two keep the energy high with their lively banter and reactions. They played two games this time, and the stream showcased their love for Aichi.
