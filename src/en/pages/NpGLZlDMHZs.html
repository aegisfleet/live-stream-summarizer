📝 Overview

This is a stream of the game 'PEAK' where ReGLOSS members (Otonose Kanade, Todoroki Hajime, Ichijou Ririka, Juufuutei Raden) are joined by Hololive's Shirakami Fubuki and Omaru Polka, working together to reach the mountain summit. It particularly depicts Polka's struggle while suffering from poison-induced hallucinations, and the members' perseverance in retrying even after multiple game overs. Ultimately, time runs out, and a survey report is displayed, but the members show enthusiasm for their next challenge.
