📝 Overview

The main part of the challenge where Hololive 6th generation members (Takane Lui, La+ Darknesss, Hakui Koyori, Kazama Iroha) tackle the fairy tale-themed horror map, "Minecraft Test of Courage 2025: Book of Märchen." This session features stages based on "The Three Little Pigs" and "Little Red Riding Hood." The members' outfits and appearances change according to the fairy tales as they face traps set by wolves and witches, and solve puzzles given by strange NPCs. Centering around Takane Lui, who is sensitive to horror, the journey is filled with constant screams, comedic retorts (tsukkomi), terror, and laughter.
