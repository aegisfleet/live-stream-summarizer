A stream where Sakura Miko challenges a test to diagnose her fear resistance. Having played horror games frequently recently, Sakura Miko tests how strong she is against fear. The highlight is watching Sakura Miko progress through the game with her inherent perseverance, even as she screams at unexpected scares. In the final test, she screams at a painting jumpscare but clears it, and her reactions to the final fear resistance results are depicted.
