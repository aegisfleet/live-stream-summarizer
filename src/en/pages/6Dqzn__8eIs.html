📝 Overview

This is a stream where AZKi plays the train conductor simulation game "TRAIN CREW." Inspired by a previous psychological test that identified "train conductor" as her ideal profession, she challenged the game again after approximately 1 year and 3 months. This time, in addition to confirming controls at the training school, she practiced driving in "free drive" mode and then took on serious driving duties in "driving (select from timetable)" mode. Especially during driving duties, she frequently struggled with adjusting stopping positions, adhering to speed limits, and managing speed on sloped routes. She also attempted driving express trains and mountain routes, and despite the high difficulty, the stream progressed in a friendly atmosphere where she enjoyed learning.
