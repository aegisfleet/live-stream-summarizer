A collaborative stream featuring VTubers Kogane Hikotora and Hibikisaki Riona playing the cooperative restaurant management game 'PlateUp!'. They reset with a simpler salad menu, introducing efficiency measures like automatic order panels and relaxing themes, but ultimately, the customers' patience ran out on Day 6, preventing them from achieving the goal of clearing Day 7. The stream was lively throughout, filled with the duo's cheerful banter and occasional shouts.
