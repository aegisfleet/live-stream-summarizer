📝 Overview

This is a special episode of the regular late-night chill radio show "Gorireba," a 3-year reunion 'call-in' project by Yukihana Lamy and Amane Kanata. A slightly nervous Lamy and Kanata engage in conversations with guests based on talk themes collected from viewers. In previous broadcasts, Kazama Iroha, Hakui Koyori, La+ Darknesss, Shishiro Botan, Inugami Korone, and Takane Lui appeared as guests, and the show was lively with questions like "What role would you play if you were family?" and "Recommended games," as well as unique questions like "Which of us would you date?". The lighthearted exchanges with the MCs kept the atmosphere throughout friendly and lively, ultimately leading to 11 Hololive members participating and exceeding expectations. Lamy, who had initially prepared for zero guests, expressed gratitude for the Hololive members' talk skills and concluded the broadcast by showing eagerness for the next session.
