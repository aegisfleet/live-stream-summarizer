📝 Overview

VTuber Todoroki Hajime takes on the management simulation game 'Onigiri Shop Simulator'. After successfully achieving profitability early on, she changes the background music to Japanese style and starts the third day of business. The new menu item, pickled plum (umeboshi), is unlocked, and she sets up an efficient system to serve three types of onigiri. However, the third day results in a slight deficit. From the fourth day onward, the shop level rises to 4, and ume onigiri becomes very popular. As customer orders increase, the struggle to skillfully prepare both the new ume onigiri and the classic sesame salt onigiri amidst the busyness is depicted. Towards the end, drinks are also unlocked, forcing her into multi-tasking between customer service and cooking. Ultimately, the shop level reaches 11, and profits are achieved, but the game ends when she runs out of rice and the shop closes due to the owner's whim.

🎨 Mood: Trial and error, careful customer service, peaceful atmosphere
