📝 Overview

A horror game stream where Houshou Marine, as a newcomer to the "Japan Incident Property Surveillance Association (JSP)", investigates incident properties through surveillance cameras. This time, she monitored three properties, challenging herself to clear both the regular and special allowance tasks for the final property, "Ibaraki Prefecture S Residence". In the regular tasks for S Residence, she calmly reported various abnormal phenomena as they occurred, earning a high evaluation and clearing the stage. She then proceeded to the special allowance tasks, and despite the increased difficulty, she accurately reported all anomalies, achieving a perfect clear. The stream maintained a tense atmosphere throughout, yet with Captain Marine's humor and precise judgment, it became a mix of fear and accomplishment.
