Overview

This is a YouTube sneak peek video for Amane Kanata's 1st solo live 'LOCK ON'. After a waiting period until the live performance begins, an introduction to the secondary merchandise, and a series of supportive messages from fans, the main part of the live footage is unveiled for the first time in the latter half of the video. As an opening act, a performance of 'Odoroki Ningen' is showcased, conveying the vibrant stage production and the excitement of the venue filled with many fans. Afterwards, during the live MC, Amane Kanata expresses gratitude to fans and introduces the band members, and the video concludes with an invitation to the paid section.

Mood: A lively atmosphere filled with anticipation and support
