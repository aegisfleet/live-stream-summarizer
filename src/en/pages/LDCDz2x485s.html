📝 Overview

A 3D off-collab stream featuring Usada Pekora and Yukihana Lamy. The "Rubber Band Watermelon Smashing" project, using the largest watermelon to date, enters its latter half. The two collaborate, sometimes competing with rock-paper-scissors, as they continue to wrap rubber bands around the watermelon. Cracks began to appear on the watermelon when the number of rubber bands exceeded 800, and it spectacularly burst when the count surpassed 1100. The dramatic transformation of the watermelon, the surprise and excitement of the two at the moment of rupture, and their shared joy of success are depicted throughout in a bright and fun atmosphere.
