📝 Overview

This stream marks Tsunomaki Watame's energetic return from a one-week hiatus and the announcement of the Minecraft event server project, 'LAMB VEGAS Presents by Watame'. A vast casino-themed space is being created within Minecraft in collaboration with Dozle's Inc. staff, and the progress is showcased during the stream. Players will compete by accumulating 'Watamedals', and the server will remain permanently accessible even after the event concludes. Listeners are invited to submit their builds, and free decoration within limits that do not spoil the scenery is also permitted. World data distribution is planned for a later date. Watame's preparations to make the event a success and her feelings as a game master are also shared, conveying a lively atmosphere full of anticipation and emphasizing that this project values unity with her fans.
