Overview

Yuzuki Choco plays the horror game "Isekai Series: Kuchisake-onna VS Ao Oni". Despite struggling with controller operations, she tries to escape from Ao Oni and Kuchisake-onna in a derelict hospital. She uses in-game items and puzzle-solving elements to disarm security, but struggles with the two monsters' skillful pursuit and warp abilities, leading to multiple game overs. Later, she teams up with new characters Akashi and Rei to aim for escape. As the game progresses, Kuchisake-onna's past and her connection to Akashi's sister, Mumu, are revealed. Ultimately, Mumu transforms into Kuchisake-onna, revealing her true identity. While the horror elements are thrilling, the streamer's witty remarks and reactions create a relaxed atmosphere during the stream.
