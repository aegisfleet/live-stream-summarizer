📝 Overview

The latter half of the dungeon cleaning game livestream featuring hololive 4th Generation members. They continue their challenging run through 'OCCULT STUDIOS'. Members struggle with increasingly complex traps and puzzles, such as moving blades, spike traps activated by floor switches, and feeding mimics, resulting in frequent death rushes and chaotic gameplay. They repeatedly trial-and-error, especially regarding using chunks of meat to disarm traps and depositing items into the Golden Chest. Finally, they solved all the gimmicks and achieved 100% level completion. After receiving rewards, they chatted while enjoying customizing each other's avatars at the shop, concluding the stream with a repeated "Thank you for visiting!" in the style of Izakaya (Japanese pub) staff.
