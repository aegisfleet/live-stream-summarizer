📝 Overview

A promotional collaboration stream for "Dragon Ball Gekishin Squadra" by the hololive unit "Suba Choco Luna Tan." They primarily played the "Gekishin Rule," a 4vs4 team battle where the goal is to defeat the enemy God of Destruction and collect Dragon Balls. In the latter half of the stream, they played a total of four matches selecting the Villain team, engaging in heated combat. Despite struggling with team KOs and having their Gods of Destruction breached, the members thoroughly enjoyed the cooperative gameplay and the unique characteristics of the characters, creating a lively atmosphere. They concluded the stream by introducing campaign information and showing enthusiasm for future attempts.
