📝 Overview

Anya Melfissa's stream playing the Japanese horror game 'Nikoniko-san'. It's an escape game based on an urban legend about an old warehouse behind a school, where Anya collects items while escaping from the creepy smiling 'Nikoniko-san'. The player explores the map and gathers information about the monster, which fears dogs. She successfully collects all the escape items: a key, chain cutters, a hammer, and a crowbar. However, the game has a loop structure, and what seemed to be the exit always leads back to the starting point. Eventually, she attempts to escape with a dog but is once again attacked by Nikoniko-san, running through an infinite loop map while fleeing from a massive number of smaller Nikoniko-sans. This time, she achieves a happy ending by escaping with the dog. After clearing the game, she enjoyed a lively chat and super chat readings with viewers.
