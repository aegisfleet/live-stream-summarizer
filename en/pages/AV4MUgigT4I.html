📝 Overview

The conclusion of "The Trial of the Magical Girl Witch." As the mastermind behind the death game, Yuki Tsukishiro (who replaced a human and executed witches), is continuously condemned, Yuki chooses self-sacrifice, casting a spell to neutralize the witch factor in herself and free her comrades. However, Mereru Hikami shields Yuki, and they are both pierced by the spear, sublimating the witch's curse. Ema Sakuraba, upon learning Yuki's true intentions, reconciles with Hiro Nikaido, who had been trying to distance himself from her. The story concludes with a touching happy ending as all the girls overcome their past traumas and return from the mansion, embracing hope for the future.
