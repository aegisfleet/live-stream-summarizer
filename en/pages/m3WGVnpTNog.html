📝 Overview

This is a stream where AZKi challenges the Kagawa Prefecture map in GeoGuessr. The rule is that if an udon shop appears, the game ends immediately. In Kagawa Prefecture, known as 'Udon Prefecture,' AZKi navigates through Takamatsu City and Sakaide City, avoiding udon shops, using calm analysis and luck to achieve perfect scores one after another. However, she repeatedly encounters udon shop signs and shops that resemble them, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful progression. Ultimately, while exploring Takamatsu City, she discovers 'Sanuki Udon Ebisu,' resulting in a game over. AZKi's reactions as she struggles to avoid udon shops until the very end are a highlight.
