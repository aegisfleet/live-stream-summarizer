📝 Overview

This is a stream where Shirakami Fubuki from Hololive challenges a solo race project in the game "R.E.P.O.". She competes with Shishiro Botan and Omaru Polka to see who can make the most progress within a 2-hour time limit. In the early stages, she struggles with repeated deaths, but from the mid-game onwards, she efficiently collects high-value items, steadily increasing her funds. In the final stages, she attempts a high-difficulty map but is again blocked by strong enemies, leading to a series of deaths. Although she ran out of time at Level 5 and did not achieve her goal, she continued to play without giving up until the very end. In the results reported by other members, Shishiro Botan won with Level 8, and Omaru Polka achieved Level 4, and they congratulated each other on their efforts.
