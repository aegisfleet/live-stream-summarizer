📝 Overview

A stream by Hololive's Natsuiro Matsuri about the new Shadowverse pack, 'Heirs of the Absolute'. This time, she challenged a battle guide and puzzle lessons to learn the mechanics of the new 'Treasure Royal' element, playing while understanding new card abilities, synergy, and fusion gimmicks. In the early stages, she was sometimes confused by unfamiliar elements but still managed to secure victories. However, in free matches from the middle onwards, she struggled against powerful opponent decks, experiencing tough battles including calculation errors and defeats by the opponent's legendary card 'Satan, the Ultimate Sin'. Despite showing frustration, the stream conveyed her curiosity and enjoyment of the game.

🎨 Mood: An atmosphere where surprise and joy are found in the game's depth, yet intertwined with frustration and sadness from difficult struggles.
