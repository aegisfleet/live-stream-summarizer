📝 Overview

A food review stream where Otonose Kanade eats the new Tsukimi Burger. In this project, which began at her manager's suggestion, it's revealed that Otonose Kanade's memories from her debut are deeply connected to the Tsukimi Burger. She recounts the emotional experience of eating a Tsukimi Burger for the first time in Japan after arriving from Korea, sharing a tearful story of ordering it. Afterward, while interacting with viewers, she unboxes a set of Tsukimi Burger, nuggets, and a drink, and performs a food review. She elaborates on the taste of the nuggets' lemon pepper sauce and mustard sauce, as well as the Tsukimi Burger itself, enjoying it while trying various arrangements. The stream maintains a consistently friendly atmosphere, with discussions about the Korean chicken burger "MOM's Touch" and moon-viewing (Otsukimi) related to the Mid-Autumn Moon festival during the food review.
