📝 Overview

A continuation of the collaborative stream where Hololive's Shirakami Fubuki, Houshou Marine, Tsunomaki Watame, and Omaru Polka gather to challenge 'Only Up!'. After overcoming the poison area, they reach a new desert region called Mesa. They attempt to clear a path using hot sand, tornadoes, cactus thorns, and dynamite, but the game's unstable physics and a series of unexpected falls repeatedly send them back to square one. Especially in this stream, from the initial poison area to the mid-point Mesa area, all members experienced frequent falls, constantly being sent back to the starting point. A thrilling development unfolded, mixing despair and laughter. Their caring for each other, occasional clumsy moments, and their unwavering determination to reach the summit despite all obstacles captivated the viewers' hearts.
