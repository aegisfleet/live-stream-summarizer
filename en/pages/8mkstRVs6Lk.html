Overview

Shiranui Flare conducted a closing stream for the Shironeko Project x Hololive collaboration, nearing its end. After resolving an audio issue at the beginning of the stream, she explained the collaboration event and talent-produced outfits, then progressed through the collaboration story with the help of her viewers. For the main content, she performed three cooperative battles, enjoying the teamwork with participants and smoothly clearing the quests. Additionally, a surprise announcement of new original illustrations of Hololive members from the Shironeko Project management was made, for which she expressed gratitude. Finally, she unveiled a partial preview of the music video for her new song 'Kirameki Mirai,' expressing thanks to her fans and her aspirations for future activities, concluding the successful stream.

Mood: During the audio trouble, there was a temporary anxious atmosphere, but after it was resolved, the mood remained consistently calm and joyful. Joy and gratitude were evident regarding the surprise illustration, and a passionate side was revealed during the introduction of her new song.
