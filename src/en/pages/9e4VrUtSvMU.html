📝 Overview

The latter half of Shirakami Fubuki's stream playing the new action RPG 'Goddess Order.' After completing the initial tutorial, she challenged the character gacha. In addition to multiple SR characters, she obtained numerous SSR characters, including the long-desired SSR Luna. She thoroughly reviewed the character development elements and skill system of the acquired characters. Particularly in SSR Luna's Knight Experience mode, she deepened her understanding of the game system by actually controlling powerful skills to fight enemies. At the end of the stream, she introduced the MV and production episodes of 'Blue Diamond,' a collaboration song with the Korean virtual artist Hebi, concluding the broadcast.
