Overview

Momosuzu Nene challenges an AI-generated true/false quiz. The rule is that she cannot leave the room until she answers 10 questions correctly in a row, aiming to clear it in 10 minutes (1 minute per question). However, the questions generated by the AI constantly overturn Nenechi's common sense, leading to repeated incorrect answers. As the levels progress, the difficulty and content of the questions become unpredictable, and Nenechi, though confused, struggles to find the correct answers. Despite repeated failures, she continues to challenge herself and finally achieves 10 consecutive correct answers, successfully escaping the room. Nenechi's never-give-up attitude until the very end is the highlight.

Mood: Confusion, struggle, and a sense of accomplishment
