Overview

A stream where streamer Sakura Miko watches and looks back on clips from Hololive's Minecraft server, "HoloServer Summer Park." She constantly burst into laughter at the fan-made clips, delving deep into the hidden talents of the members and the behind-the-scenes of the event. With moments of explosive laughter, emotion, and confusion, Sakura Miko's emotions were richly expressed, making it a fun time to share summer memories. In the middle section, Sakura Miko was continuously bursting into laughter and confusion, especially during the HoloMembers' "M-side" experience and the intense exchange of lines in the "pig-teasing" attraction. The screams and counterattacks of the HoloMembers who turned into pigs, followed by merciless taunts impersonated by the "S-side" HoloMembers, led to a chaotic yet hilarious development. In the latter half, she was further at the mercy of more extreme rollercoaster and observatory prank clips, unable to stop laughing at the continuous stream of bizarre ideas and brutal attractions. Towards the end, she excitedly talked about her experience watching a Harry Potter stage play, conveying her deep impression of the stage direction and the wonderful cast. To conclude the stream, she announced details about the clip video's description and archive release, and expressed gratitude to the viewers.

Mood: A consistently bright and lively atmosphere filled with laughter. Many moments of surprise and emotion.
