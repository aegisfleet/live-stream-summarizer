📝 Overview

Hololive 4th Generation members (Shirakami Fubuki, Houshou Marine, Shiranui Flare, Tsunomaki Watame) take on the challenge of "PEAK" in PICO PARK. They overcome various difficulties such as survival on a deserted island, poisonous gas, and giant beehives, advancing to the desert stage. There, they face new trials like intense sunlight, tornadoes, cactus thorns, and divided routes. At times, they are disoriented by hallucinations or suffer from exhaustion, yet they believe in teamwork and aim for the summit in this lively and thrilling adventure log. In the desert stage, new threats like scorpions and lava appear. Even when separated, they help each other and secure food as they proceed. In the final phase, they cooperatively overcome the lava zone and challenge the final stage of "PEAK," but unfortunately, not all members were able to reach the goal. However, it was a positive stream, showing eagerness to challenge again, with anticipation for new items unlocked after the game over and the next new project, "Card Pack Appraisal Battle."

🎨 Mood: Lively and fun, at times thrilling, adventurous atmosphere, and anticipation for the next challenge.
