📝 Overview

As the manager of the TCG shop 'Miko's Hole,' streamer Sakura Miko aims to counteract the inflation of other shops due to an update by obtaining cards worth over 10 million. This time, the focus is on improving shop management efficiency, repeatedly appraising high-value cards, buying and selling packs, while promoting automation through the installation of new appraisal machines and giving instructions to part-time staff. The video depicts her increasing the shop's sales, sometimes refusing to buy low-value cards or being puzzled by her staff's actions. Towards the end, she acquires multiple 'Gem Mint 10' cards through high-value appraisals, moving a step closer to her goal.

🎨 Mood: A lively atmosphere, starting with an upbeat mood, then feeling a sense of crisis due to other shops' inflation, and striving towards the goal. Sakura Miko's occasional assertive remarks, surprise, and confused reactions are entertaining. Highlights include her struggles with optimizing management simulation and her excitement when acquiring high-value cards.
