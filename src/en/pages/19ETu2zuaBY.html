📝 Overview

A collaboration stream featuring Sakura Miko gathering Hololive members to play 'Among Us VR,' the 3D version of Among Us. The theme of the stream was '3D Among Us full of blunders (Pon),' resulting in a chaotic and lively development throughout, where clumsy actions and skillful plays by the Imposters/Infectious side intertwined. Especially in the latter half, La+ Darknesss and Oozora Subaru showed overwhelming strength on the Imposter side, dominating the discussions. In the final stage, Zombie Mode (Infection rules) was introduced. Although the Crewmates aimed to complete tasks, the game quickly ended due to the fierce assault from the Infected side. Ultimately, the Infected team claimed victory, concluding the highly energetic stream.
