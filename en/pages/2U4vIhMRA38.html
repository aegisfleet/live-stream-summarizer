📝 Overview

A stream where Kikirara Vivi challenges the game "Oshaberi King!" (Chatting King!), which tests communication skills using a microphone across various situations. She is required to use appropriate speaking styles and emotional expressions when addressing a wide range of people, including teachers, students, family, bosses, and partners. Vivi struggled with nervousness and speaking too fast in the initial stages but gradually got the hang of it, earning high evaluations through emotional speeches and thoughtful conversations. In the middle stages and beyond, she tackled difficult challenges such as proposals, consulting with a boss, and refusing a confession. Towards the end, she even attempted highly challenging situations like apology press conferences, striving to improve her communication skills. She ultimately cleared all stages and shared her thoughts as the stream concluded.
