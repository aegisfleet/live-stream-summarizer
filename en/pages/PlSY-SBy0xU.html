📝 Overview

Akai Haato and Hyakumantenbara Salome collaborated to challenge the mountain climbing game "PEAK". Network issues occurred at the beginning, and several attempts to reconnect failed. Afterward, they successfully started the game and overcame various obstacles aiming for the summit. Along the way, they assisted each other, progressing through cooperative play with humorous exchanges and mishaps. In the middle, they returned to an airport-like area to check their journey records and even changed outfits for a change of pace before retrying. They continued to gather food from palm trees, discover new items, and cooperatively overcome difficult areas like dark caves and dense poisonous plants. However, due to repeated poison damage and hunger, they fell many times, resulting in a game over right before clearing the game. They played positively without giving up until the very end, making for a fun stream filled with laughter.
