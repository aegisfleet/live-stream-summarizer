Overview

Otonose Kanade, a member of Hololive DEV_IS's ReGLOSS, hosted a collaborative stream for "R.E.P.O." with guests Tsunomaki Watame and Shigure Ui. In this horror treasure-hunting game, players collect items to repair a spaceship and strengthen themselves with money earned at delivery points. In the beginning, they struggled with controls but cooperated to collect items, though game-overs were frequent. The stream was full of exciting moments, including a large number of enemies appearing during deliveries, the emergence of a giant Headman, and a unique situation where cute duck-like enemies could be flushed down the toilet for cash. While the atmosphere remained generally peaceful and lively, there were also occasional heart-pounding moments. As the difficulty increased and strategic movements were required, the players progressed through the stages with trial and error. Eventually, in the new stage "SWIFTBROOM ACADEMY," despite encountering more powerful enemies and traps, they successfully achieved all objectives and cleared the stage.

Mood: Generally peaceful and lively throughout, with occasional thrilling moments
