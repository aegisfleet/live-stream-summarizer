📝 Overview

In the 9th episode of "Gorileba Radio", Amane Kanata and Yukihana Lamy looked back at their realistic experiences with a GTA car accident, which was a hot topic in the previous broadcast, and talked about its impact. They also deeply discussed the swift passage of time as an adult and how their VTuber activities affect their sense of time. As the number of Hololive members continues to increase, they revealed their self-awareness as seniors, their conflicts in relationships with juniors, and their feelings about the changes in their environment since their debut. Furthermore, they shared their struggle with not being good at continuing things and explored ways to deal with it. They also touched upon health concerns such as chronic stiff shoulders and "straight neck" syndrome, and discussed stress relief and motivation maintenance methods suggested by viewers. Finally, they had lively discussions on a wide range of topics, including how to balance work and leisure, especially the importance and ways to spend off days, deep conversations about each other's fashion and health, behind-the-scenes stories from HoloWitch recordings, and expectations for future Gorileba projects.
