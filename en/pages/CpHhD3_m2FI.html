Overview

In the latter half of Momosuzu Nene's 5th anniversary stream, she continued to challenge her first "reverse raid" project despite various mishaps. She overcame a comment section freeze and announced 5th-anniversary merchandise and a new song. Afterward, she reverse-raided Vivi (Vestia Zeta) from Hololive Indonesia, leading to lively discussions on various talk themes. Throughout the entire stream, Nene's bright and energetic character and her warm interactions with viewers, peers, and seniors were depicted, making it a highly successful anniversary stream.

Mood: Consistently bright and lively, conveying Nene's nervousness while also fostering a sense of unity with viewers and guests. A festive atmosphere filled with surprises and emotion.
