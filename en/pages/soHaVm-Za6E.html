📝 Overview

This is a stream where Hololive's Tsunomaki Watame challenges the cooperative game 'R.E.P.O.' with Todoki Hajime and Shigure Ui. It's a survival horror game where they collect treasures to earn money for spaceship repairs. Despite Shigure Ui's overly free actions as a first-time player and being surprised by sudden enemies, the three cooperate to progress through the game. Notably, they overcome unexpected incidents and fears, such as unique ideas like flushing enemies down the toilet, a shrinking phenomenon, and battles with giant gnomes. They scream at the appearance of various enemies but work together to collect items and repeatedly upgrade. Ultimately, they show persistence by clearing the mission even when everyone is defeated, leading to a moving ending where they repair the spaceship and embark on a new journey.

🎨 Mood: A lively atmosphere filled with constant laughter, occasionally punctuated by screams from horror elements, and a continuous series of cooperative play and unexpected events. There are many scenes that induce laughter even in desperate situations, ultimately leading to a sense of accomplishment and emotion.
