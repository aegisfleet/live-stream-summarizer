📝 Overview

This is the music video for Shirogane Noel's original song "Très Bien Night". Set in a retro diner, it depicts two sides of Noel: Noel as a waitress burdened by work, and Noel as a free spirit who has unleashed her desires. Although she initially shows distress from her overwhelming daily life, she gradually confronts herself and releases her inner passion, ultimately singing and dancing joyfully in a radiant form. This song and video convey a message of self-affirmation and liberation.
