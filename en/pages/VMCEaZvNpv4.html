📝 Overview

This stream is a continuation of the horror game 'I Want to Scare Shishiro Botan Vol. 2 ~Japan Accident Property Surveillance Association (JSP) Arc~', hosted by Hololive's Shishiro Botan, with Inugami Korone and Takane Lui as guests. In this game, players find and report anomalies from surveillance camera footage. This time, there were many missed anomalies, frequently resulting in a 0 yen salary. The members were puzzled, asking 'Where's the anomaly?' and 'I can't see it!', but also screamed and gasped when encountering scary objects like 'an old man's head,' 'a dog,' 'a clown,' 'a white face,' and 'a group of cats on the roof.' With the help of listener comments, they reduced their misses and eventually completed surveillance tasks for the S Residence in Ibaraki Prefecture, earning a salary more often. However, the stream drifted a bit from the original purpose of scaring Shishiro Botan, becoming a more relaxed horror collaboration. Towards the end, Shishiro Botan herself admitted the project was a 'failure,' stating that next time Takane Lui will choose a game, seeking one with more realistic and human-centric horror. Amidst the cheerful atmosphere, anticipation for the next stream grew.
