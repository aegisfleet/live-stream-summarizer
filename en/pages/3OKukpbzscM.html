📝 Overview

Natsuiro Matsuri played a horror game called "Japan Property Accident Surveillance Association." The game is based on the operations of a real company, and a warning is issued for those with weak hearts or under 18 years old to refrain from playing. It's a game where you identify and report "abnormal phenomena" appearing in rooms or outdoors while switching between surveillance camera feeds. Matsuri sometimes expressed surprise, but also made witty comments about the mundane phenomena, progressing through the game while humorously narrating the highlights. In the first property, she showed a mixed reaction of surprise and laughter to the more clearly appearing abnormal phenomena, eventually clearing the stage and earning a reward. Afterward, surveillance began on a newly unlocked property, "Ibaraki Prefecture S Residence," and she once again took on the challenge of finding abnormal phenomena.
