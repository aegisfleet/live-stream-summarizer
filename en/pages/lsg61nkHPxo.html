📝 Overview

This stream featured Ichijo Ririka opening a large number of packs from Shadowverse's new expansion, "Heirs of the Cataclysm," and then constructing a deck with the cards she obtained. In the beginning, she passionately discussed campaign information and her own gameplay status, particularly her struggles with the Witch class. She then got very excited while opening numerous packs, acquiring legendary cards and a premium version of "Mazelbane, Absolute Incarnation." In the latter half, she attempted to build a Dragon deck using the new cards and challenged a ranked match with it, but narrowly lost. However, she felt a strong potential in the Mazelbane deck and vowed to practice more and challenge again in future streams. The atmosphere was consistently amicable, with in-depth discussions about card illustrations, effects, and deck possibilities, concluding with an announcement for a collaboration stream.
