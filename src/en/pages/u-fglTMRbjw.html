📝 Overview

A collaboration stream where Hololive members play Werewolf RPG in Minecraft. Set in a luxurious Western-style mansion, numerous members, including Tsunomaki Watame, participate. Through gathering items in the night turns and discussions in the day turns, they aim to identify the werewolves and engage in a game of deception for survival. Unpredictable developments and mutual suspicion among members continued throughout, with the vampires winning the 3rd match and the werewolves winning the final 4th match, creating an intense battle until the very end. New roles, 'Wolf-attached Villager' and 'Baker,' were also added, deepening the game.
