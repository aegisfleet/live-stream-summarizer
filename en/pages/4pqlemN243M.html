📝 Overview

A project where Hololive 5th Generation member Omaru Polka decides the most delicious convenience store hot snack. She discusses her favorite hot snacks with the staff, and after deliberating between 'Pizza Man' and 'American Dog', 'Pizza Man' was ultimately chosen as No.1. It's an audience-participation project that funnily explores which hot snacks are delicious.

🎨 Mood: A consistently friendly atmosphere, with a strong passion for food.
