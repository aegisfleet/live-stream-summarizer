📝 Overview

Riona Hibisaki and guest Matsuri-senpai took on the cooperative climbing game "PEAK" for the first time. Stranded on a deserted island after a plane crash, they collected items and aimed for the summit under Matsuri-senpai's precise instructions. Despite repeated mishaps like poisonous plants, falls from cliffs, and game overs, they continued their climb in a harmonious atmosphere. After 40 minutes, Matsuri-senpai even transformed into a mysterious white ghost. Afterward, they progressed through new stages such as "Coast," "Tropics (Day 2)," "Mesa (Desert)," "Caldera (Day 6)," and "Crater (Day 7)," facing new dangers like sunlight, scorpions, and TNT, but never giving up on reaching the summit. This time, they successfully reached the summit and cleared the game.
