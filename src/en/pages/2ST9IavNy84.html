📝 Overview

A collaboration stream between Oozora Subaru and Sakura Miko, where they played the bomb defusal game 'Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes'. This game involves viewers deciphering a manual and giving instructions to the bomb defuser. The two switched roles midway through, tackling bomb defusal amidst various mishaps. After achieving their goal of three successful defusals, they challenged an even more difficult 8-module, 8-minute bomb. Despite being bewildered by numerous modules, they showed unwavering cooperation until the end, leading to a continuous stream of excitement and laughter. Especially in the final 'Fair Game', they struggled with complex conditions and time limits but persevered, ultimately succeeding in defusing the bomb. It was a moving finale that showcased their bond and growth.
