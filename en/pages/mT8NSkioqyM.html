📝 Overview

Shishiro Botan experiences a pre-release play of 'Resident Evil Requiem'. She explains the tense introduction where the protagonist Grace starts by being hung upside down, and the innovative game system that allows real-time switching between first-person and third-person perspectives. Set in an abandoned hotel (sanatorium), the game features thrilling exploration while escaping eerie enemies that react to sounds. While surprised by the high quality of the visuals and horror direction, she also explains the benefits of perspective switching for players who are not good at action games. She expresses her anticipation for the future development of the game and her desire to share impressions with viewers.
