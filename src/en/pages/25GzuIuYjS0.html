📝 Overview

This is a stream where Hololive's Hakui Koyori reviews past matches of the Holo Minecraft Werewolf RPG, providing detailed rule explanations and observing the members' gameplay. She thoroughly explained the winning conditions for each role, such as Villager, Werewolf, Accomplice, and Vampire, as well as actions during day and night turns, and items that can be bought with emeralds. This session focused on the structure of the mansion serving as the game's setting, changes to items, and updates to roles. Additionally, she delved into strategies for the new map and the effectiveness of various items, especially invisibility potions. As an organizer, Hakui Koyori worked on adjusting the game balance while interacting with comments from viewers, building anticipation for the next event. The audience was excited by the tense game developments from each member's perspective and the surprising outcomes, creating a consistently pleasant atmosphere and high expectations for the next installment.
