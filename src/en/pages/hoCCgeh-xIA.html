📝 Overview

This is the second half of Hololive's "Fully Live 3D Acoustic Live in Summer." Starting with "Garnet," which resumed after an interruption due to a comment glitch, the live show featured "Uchiage Hanabi" by Mori Calliope and AZKi, and then all performers sang "secret base ~Kimi ga Kureta Mono~," creating an emotional finale. Between each song, there were relaxed talks with guests and screenshot opportunities, where the preparation for the entire live show and thoughts on the songs were shared. This live show is characterized by immersive performances with fully live 3D and live music.
