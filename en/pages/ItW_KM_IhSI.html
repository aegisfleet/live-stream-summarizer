📝 Overview

The second half of the gorilla game, started to resolve the feeling of incompleteness left by Elden Ring. However, a miraculous burst of concentration finally allowed her to cross the bridge. Feeling unsatisfied, she tackled the bonus stage, "Lunar Battle," where she became a spacesuit gorilla, using megaton punches to effortlessly defeat Apollo program astronauts. The stream concluded with a triumphant finale, wrapping up the approximately 13-hour broadcast.
