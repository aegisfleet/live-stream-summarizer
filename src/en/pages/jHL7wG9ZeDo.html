📝 Overview

This is the second installment of a project where Hololive members are envisioned as countries and simulated using Fantasy Map Simulator. This time, 'Konkoyo Empire' starts in a 'besieged on all sides' state, surrounded by 13 neighboring countries. Ultimately, the Konkoyo Empire faces an unexpected outcome: despite being encircled by powerful nations, it survives by becoming a vassal state of another country (Franconia Federation). The streamer, while shocked by this 'dirty way of survival,' rejoiced in its ultimate survival and expressed anticipation for the next project.

🎨 Mood: The stream maintained a consistently cheerful atmosphere, with constant ups and downs, laughter, and surprises due to the unpredictable developments of the simulation.
