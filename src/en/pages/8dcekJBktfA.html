📝 Overview

A spontaneous singing stream by AZKi. She captivated listeners with her high vocal ability and expressiveness, performing covers of emotional anime songs, J-POP, and Vocaloid tracks. Highlights included performing Ai Higuchi's 'Akuma no Ko' (The Child of Evil), ZUTOMAYO's 'Tadashiku Narenai' (Can't Be Right), and Yorushika's 'Shayou' (Setting Sun). In the latter half, she sang her original song 'Aoi Yume' (Blue Dream), and concluded the stream strongly with an encore performance of Chihiro Onitsuka's 'Gekkou' (Moonlight). She also announced her live concert 'Departure' and discussed the graduation of hololive EN's Tsukumo Sana. The stream ended with her thanking the viewers before moving to Super Chat reading.
