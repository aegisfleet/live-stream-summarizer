📝 Overview

The fourth day of Usada Pekora's one-week project. This time, La+ Darknesss was in charge of the project, hosting a "Popularity Battle" to find true friends. The goal was to test how readily Hololive members would agree to a sudden collaboration invitation late at night. AZKi, Yukihana Lamy, Hoshimachi Suisei, Yuzuki Choco, Nekomata Okayu, Shirogane Noel, Nekota Himari, Oozora Subaru, and Shirakami Fubuki all readily accepted, making the project a great success. Pekora and La+ were both surprised by the miraculous result, where everyone they called eventually agreed to participate. An unexpected invitation was also extended to Anju Katrina, but Nenechi (Momosuzu Nene) ultimately participated instead, resulting in a tie in the Popularity Battle. This stream also included the unveiling of La+ and Pekora's new outfits and announcements for future projects.
