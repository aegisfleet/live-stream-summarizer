📝 Overview

FUWAMOCO held a simultaneous viewing stream of the Hololive 8th Anniversary Fireworks Festival "Shiny Sparklers Symphony." Even after the free public section ended, they continued to be moved by the magnificent display of fireworks launched to a medley of songs by Hololive JP, ID, and EN members, and the grand production of member logos and oshi marks drawn across the night sky. In the final segment, "Kira meki Rider!" played as an encore, bringing the fireworks festival to its climax. In this final analysis range, they repeatedly expressed deep gratitude for the 8th-anniversary milestone, were once again impressed by the perfect production of the fireworks show synchronized with music, and the staff's efforts. They powerfully spoke of the bond with fans and the anticipation for the future of "Unstoppable Hololive," concluding an emotional stream with thanks to their viewers.
