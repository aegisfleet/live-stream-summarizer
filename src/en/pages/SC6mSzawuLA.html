📝 Overview

This is a horror project where Akai Haato rides a "Ghost Taxi" and tours various haunted spots in Tokyo. The driver, despite hating haunted places, gets involved in a special tour guiding customers with psychic abilities. They visit a railway crossing where the tragedy of World War II lingers, a bus stop rumored to be the site of a woman's sad end, and a tunnel known as the most terrifying in Kanto, hearing frightening stories related to each spot. Along the way, mysterious phenomena such as Akai Haato's light spontaneously changing colors occur, making it a chilling experience from beginning to end.
