📝 Overview

Anya Melfissa of Hololive ID Generation 2 and guest Pavolia Reine streamed the final episode of the cooperative game "Split Fiction." In a unique setting where a futuristic city and a fantasy world are divided left and right, they tackled puzzles and action sequences while controlling their respective characters. Although initially struggling with the controls due to not having played for a while, they gradually coordinated to overcome complex gimmicks and boss battles as the game progressed. Towards the end, there were mechanics where the left and right screens swapped, and characters were drawn into the game world, which they overcame through cooperation. Finally, they engaged in a climactic battle with the giant boss "Ladder" and reached a moving ending.

🎨 Mood: A consistently cheerful atmosphere throughout, with cooperative gameplay filled with laughter despite occasional mishaps. The final act was concluded with a thrilling boss battle and an emotional ending.
