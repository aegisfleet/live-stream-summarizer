📝 Overview

A casual chat stream where Hoshimachi Suisei recounts her memories of the 'Zen'in Yuusho! VICTORY25' collaborative live event in Nagoya. She excitedly looked back on performing with Sambomaster, the electrifying atmosphere of the venue, and the sense of unity with the fans. She passionately spoke about Takashi Yamaguchi's moving episode, performing live without much rehearsal, playing without a click track, and the joy of singing 'Hikari no Rock.' The stream also covered a wide range of topics, including the announcement of her appearance at Coca-Cola X-Fes, her delight at being selected for Forbes Japan's '30 Under 30,' a microphone mishap, congratulations for AZKi's solo live, her ambition for new solo songs, her sightseeing experience in Nagoya, and her thoughts on a puzzle-solving event, enjoying interaction with her fans throughout.
