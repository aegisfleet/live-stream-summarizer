📝 Overview

This was the first Asa Mio (Morning Mio) stream of October. Mio worked all night preparing for the previous day's recording and approached the stream running on almost no sleep, resulting in a sleepy tone throughout. For her recent updates, she passionately discussed her progress in "Eikan Nine," a baseball simulation game she is hooked on off-stream, where she is currently raising a powerful high school team. Afterward, she introduced the theme collected on Twitter: "#WarmMealsToEatInAutumn." The latter half featured "Asa Mio News," covering various Hololive announcements (miComet/FubuMio new outfits, Tokino Sora live concert, merchandise info, etc.). She also touched upon the sad news of Haruzaki Nodoka's retirement and Hizaki Ao's graduation, sending them positive encouragement. Finally, she announced that she is considering taking an extended break in November.
