📝 Overview

This is a stream where Sakura Miko plays Werewolf RPG in Minecraft with other Hololive members. The map for this session is a Western-style mansion, and the members each have roles, cooperating or deceiving each other. Miko becomes a werewolf on the first day and starts attacking other members. On the first night, no one is defeated, and day breaks. On the second night, Koyori becomes a victim. On the third night, Miko is defeated by a vampire, but the vampire is also defeated by another member. During the daytime meeting on the fourth day, Miko is exposed as a werewolf and forced to leave, but the werewolf team wins because accomplices remain. In this game, Miko participates as a villager and uses her fortune teller ability to search for werewolves. Suspicion falls on La+ Darknesss and AZKi, but ultimately, AZKi is revealed to be a vampire, and the game concludes with the vampire team's victory.
