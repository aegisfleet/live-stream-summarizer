Overview

A 3D collaboration stream featuring Nekomata Okayu and Oozora Subaru. They appeared in summer-themed marine-style outfits and played Nintendo Switch Sports. They challenged various sports including soccer, volleyball, badminton, bowling, chambara (sword fighting), tennis, and golf. Throughout the stream, they engaged in dynamic movements and reactions with their 3D models, lively banter, and thoroughly enjoyed the games in a friendly atmosphere. The winner and loser were decided in the final game, with the loser performing squats as a penalty. The stream concluded with a commemorative photo session and words of gratitude.
