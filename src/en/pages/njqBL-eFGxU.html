📝 Overview

The second part of 'Out-of-Office Rapo-Lab: Bizen Ware Edition' commemorates Juufuutei Raden's 2nd debut anniversary. Under the guidance of traditional craftsman Sensei Takahiro Hosokawa, they experience the process of making clay from raw earth and challenge themselves to create pottery using an electric potter's wheel. Furthermore, they visit Professor Yoshihiro Kusano at Okayama University of Science to scientifically explore the mechanism behind the vibrant colors of Bizen ware's 'Hidasuki' and 'Kinsai'. They uncover the beauty of kiln variations created by oxidation and reduction, and the secrets of 'epsilon-type iron oxide,' which is difficult to produce artificially. The participants experience the craftsmanship, philosophy, and scientific depth embedded in Bizen ware, rediscovering its charm. After the on-location shoot, they unwind at a hotel incorporating Bizen ware, reflecting on the experience, and conclude with gratitude to the performers and collaborators, along with announcements of future activities.
