The 7th episode of the late-night radio show "Aki Choco Night Palette," featuring Hololive's Yuzuki Choco (Choco-sensei) and Aki Rosenthal (Aki-chan). The theme for this episode was listener submissions about "Times when a complex unexpectedly came in handy." The broadcast started with the hosts sharing recent events (Choco-sensei losing her credit card, Aki Rosenthal's complex about her soft voice), followed by reading listener anecdotes related to their complexes. Many stories involved complexes leading to positive outcomes, creating a time filled with laughter, empathy, and healing. Choco-sensei also provided a comforting phrase at the end.
