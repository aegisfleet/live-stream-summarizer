Overview

Omaru Polka's Among Us collaboration stream. Hololive members, along with guest Chihaya Kurono, take on a werewolf-style game in space. From the beginning, incidents continue, and they are at the mercy of Imposters with the special role of Shapeshifter. Polka often draws the Imposter role, skillfully outsmarting the Crewmates with clever strategies, but sometimes also making accidental blunders involving allies. Furthermore, the stream is packed with content that entertains viewers, such as a rare play session where everyone is an Imposter, and an introduction to Among Us's new feature 'Cosmicubes'. It's a highlight-filled stream with thrilling psychological battles and hilarious developments.

Mood: The atmosphere is consistently friendly, with many moments of laughter due to unpredictable developments. The interactions between the streamer and guests are enjoyable.
