📝 Overview

The 4th episode of the late-night radio show hosted by Hololive's 'big sister' type, Aki Rosenthal and Yuzuki Choco-sensei. This time, with the theme 'What I secretly do in the middle of the night,' they discuss listener submissions and their own experiences. Various 'secret activities' were revealed, such as Choco-sensei's first cup yakisoba experience, an episode where her card was stopped due to buying too many manga, listeners' fan activities (can badge decoration and Holo card research), and even filming a hamster's nocturnal activities. The broadcast concludes with Choco-sensei's sweet goodnight message, providing a relaxing and healing atmosphere.

🎨 Mood: A consistently peaceful and amusing adult-oriented radio show, eliciting quiet chuckles.
