📝 Overview

A collaborative stream where Yukihana Lamy, Omaru Polka, and Momosuzu Nene challenge the mountain climbing game 'PEAK'. Set in diverse environments like caves and deserts, they struggle with poison traps and food shortages, but strategically use items and cooperate to progress. However, in this exploration, they got lost in a deep cave and suffered from obstacles like bombs and scorpions, ultimately leading Polka to succumb, turn into bones, and cause a game over. For a new challenge, they restarted at an airport, checked their passports, looked back at their past adventures, and built up anticipation for their next journey.
