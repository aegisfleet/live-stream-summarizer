Overview

A 5th-anniversary 3D live concert by Hololive unit 'Nepochka'. The event unfolded as a story where they decide on performances for a school dorm festival, entertaining viewers with various productions like group songs in school uniforms, band performances, and poolside swimsuit lives. In the latter half of the concert, after introducing 5th-anniversary merchandise, the members performed a medley of their representative and original songs as the dorm festival finale. Furthermore, a new song, 'Hitotsu ni Naru Koe' (A Voice Becoming One), was surprisingly announced, bringing a moving conclusion. The concert was filled with heartwarming interactions among the members and the charm of their singing and dancing.

Mood: Consistently bright and fun atmosphere, conveying the good camaraderie among members.
