📝 Overview

A project where Shishiro Botan hosts her first 'gyaku-totsu' (reverse call-in) stream. This time, with the talk theme 'Things I want to do with Shishiro Botan,' she made reverse call-ins to HoloMembers, soliciting 'things they want to do together.' With the simple rule of only one talk theme, Shishiro Botan intends to fulfill HoloMembers' suggestions as much as possible. While previous reverse call-ins often involved ramen-related suggestions, this time a wide variety of plans were proposed, including humorous ASMR exchanges with Nekomata Okayu, a physical activity collaboration with Hiodoshi Ao, and a promise of ramen guidance and a hot spring trip with Todoroki Hajime. Towards the end of the stream, her birthday 3D live, 'SHISHIRO BOTAN BIRTHDAY 3D LIVE must R&LIVE,' was also announced.

🎨 Mood: A consistently amicable, lively, and enjoyable atmosphere.
