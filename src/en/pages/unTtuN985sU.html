Overview

Takane Lui's summer horse racing prediction stream. At the beginning of the video, various Hololive merchandise announcements are played. When the stream begins, Takane Lui reveals that her throat is not in good condition but proceeds to start her main horse race predictions for 'Sapporo Kinen' and 'Chukyo Kinen'. Especially for 'Chukyo Kinen', she provides detailed analysis and opinions on each participating horse, making passionate predictions based on jockey and horse characteristics, past race results, weight, and more. Despite the difficulty of the predictions, she agonizingly decides on her bets. Afterwards, the results for the Chukyo Kinen and Sapporo Kinen races are announced, and Takane Lui is overcome with emotion and joy at Top Knife's victory, expressing explosive excitement. She expresses gratitude to jockey Norihiro Yokoyama and shows surprise and happiness at her prediction coming true. The joy of victory makes her completely disregard her losses for the day, and while laughing that she's gone crazy from celebrating, she finally thanks her viewers and concludes the stream in the best possible mood.

Mood: Calm but serious, excited and joyful
