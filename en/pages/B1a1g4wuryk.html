📝 Overview

A stream where Hololive's Sakura Miko makes predictions and provides live commentary for the overseas G1 race, the "Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe 2025." Just before the race, guest Umezaki-san joined to confirm their predictions before purchasing the final betting tickets. In addition to her favorite, "Alohi Ahia," the long shot "Daly's" she purchased achieved a dramatic victory, resulting in successful wins for the win (single) and place (show) bets. Still buzzing from the win, she discussed the novelty of the information gathering process and the depth of horse racing before concluding the stream in joy.
