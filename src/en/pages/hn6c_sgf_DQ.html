Overview

A Shadowverse ranked match stream. Natsuiro Matsuri, aiming for promotion to Rank B, plays by overcoming game data linking issues and leveraging the results of her off-stream coaching. Ultimately, she navigates through intense developments, finally achieving promotion to B0 rank and exploding with joy. This stream highlights Natsuiro Matsuri's positive attitude, showcasing her sense of accomplishment from the rank-up and her commitment to further growth.
