📝 Overview

Usada Pekora's 6th-anniversary 3D live was an innovative production themed around pachislot, featuring an animated opening and various pachislot-style effects interspersed between songs. In addition to solo performances, duets and collaborative songs with a lineup of luxurious guests such as Tokoyami Towa, Shishiro Botan, Oozora Subaru, Houshou Marine, Nekomata Okayu, Amane Kanata, Inugami Korone, IRyS, Yozora Mel, Shirakami Fubuki, Nakiri Ayame, La+ Darknesss, Shiranui Flare, and Shirogane Noel were successively showcased. The live concluded with all guests rejoining for a grand performance that electrified the venue. It was a celebratory and lively anniversary live, fitting for her 6th year.
