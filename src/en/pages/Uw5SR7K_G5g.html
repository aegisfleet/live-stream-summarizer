Overview

Shiori Novella, joined by guests HACHAMA and IRyS, takes on the hiking segment of the survival game 'Only Up!'. Restarting after a previous failure, they progress while securing food like coconuts and marshmallows, but dangers such as fog, poisonous plants, and scorchberries beset the members. In a chaotic turn of events where HACHAMA and IRyS successively retire, Shiori repeatedly faces difficulties alone. Although she revives her companions at a campfire, retirements continue due to poisonous plants again. Shiori aims for the summit alone without giving up, but new threats like desert heat, tornadoes, and scorpions now stand in her way. Eventually, Shiori also succumbs to food shortage and dangerous creatures, leading to a third collective retirement. The video depicts their struggle as they repeatedly fall into desperate situations but restart without giving up.

Mood: A sudden shift from peaceful to a chaotic atmosphere mixed with impatience and resignation
