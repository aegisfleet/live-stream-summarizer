📝 Overview

Four Hololive members (Shirakami Fubuki, Natsuiro Matsuri, Yukihana Lamy, Kazama Iroha) played the co-op simulation game "Keep Digging," where they dig holes to unearth buried ores, fossils, and treasures. Early in the game, they turned into monkeys and continued digging deep underground in that form. They broke through the ruins at 600m underground, the ancient temple at 700m, and the lava zone past 900m, finally reaching the underground bunker at 1000m. The stream concluded with great excitement after a shocking ending where pressing the final button reset the world.
