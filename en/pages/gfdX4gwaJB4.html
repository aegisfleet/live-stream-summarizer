📝 Overview

A stream by Hololive's Usada Pekora, covering her final prediction for the Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe, watching the race, and announcing the results. Based on past data and immediate odds changes, she purchased a cheering ticket for a Japanese horse (Alohealy, Place bet, ¥3,000) and main prediction tickets for international horses expected to win (Sojay and Mini Hawk, Place bets, ¥100,000 each). She watched the race unfolding while shouting, and ultimately, her Place bets on the two favorite international horses hit. She achieved a major victory (¥510,000 payout on a ¥203,000 investment), recovering nearly all of her losses from the previous year's disastrous Arc de Triomphe. She conveyed her excitement and gratitude. At the end of the stream, she declared that this win would be the starting point for a "positive life" and expressed enthusiasm for future horse racing streams.
