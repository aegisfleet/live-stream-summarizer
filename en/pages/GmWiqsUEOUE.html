📝 Overview

Sakura Miko, Shirakami Fubuki, and Oozora Subaru from Hololive challenged a listener-participation survival project in Mario Kart World. Their goal was to secure first place within two hours and dedicate it to Ookami Mio. From the start, they faced continuous struggles due to listeners' clever item attacks and unfortunate circumstances. In the middle and later stages, despite trying various characters, they were met with frustrating last-minute reversals and fierce competition, making it difficult to reach their target of first place. Towards the end, the three aimed for a top position through cooperative play, but strong interference from listeners and their own mistakes prevented them from getting first place. Their final sprint was in vain, and they eventually ran out of time, resulting in the penalty game of uninstalling the game. Watching the game disappear, the three expressed their regret, but also conveyed gratitude to their listeners, enthusiasm for future activities, and renewed messages of support for Mio, bringing the stream to a close.
