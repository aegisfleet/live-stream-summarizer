📝 Overview

Akirose's Mahjong stream, where she aimed not to deal into opponents' wins as a penalty for the "Mahjong Soul Uprising League." She declared her theme for the stream to be "getting used to open tanyao and not dealing into opponents' wins," but she ended up dealing into a mangan (full house) win with yakuhai hon'itsu (honor tiles all of one suit) and a riichi dora 1 (ready hand with one dora tile) during the game, failing to achieve her goal. However, her composure in analyzing the situation and her persistent approach to the game remained unchanged. In the middle of the stream, she even rose to first place with a splendid win, engaging in close battles. Although she dealt into opponents' hands again towards the end, Akirose's sincere dedication to mahjong throughout the stream, occasionally showing her playful side, made for a memorable broadcast.
