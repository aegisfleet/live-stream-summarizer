📝 Overview

This is a special edition of 'Holo no Kyujitsu' (Holo's Holiday), where Hololive announcer Harutsaki Nodoka welcomes guest Hakos Baelz to challenge projects recruited through HoloPlus. Following Baelz's unique self-introduction and announcements of major Hololive EN and ID concerts, the main segment involves tasting Vegemite, known as the 'world's worst jam,' combined with various Japanese dishes. Unexpectedly delicious combinations were discovered one after another, with Vegemite miso soup and Vegemite sushi receiving surprisingly high praise. A chaotic yet lively food review talk unfolded throughout, concluding with a Japanese quiz and a behind-the-scenes segment about the stream.
