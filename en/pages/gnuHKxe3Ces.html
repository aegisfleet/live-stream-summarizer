📝 Overview

Kiara attempted an ASMR stream centered on sharpening her kitchen knives using the high-end German roller knife sharpener, "HORL 2". After spending time adjusting the complex handcam and microphone setup, she began sharpening the knives using the magnetic angle support. Despite struggling with the sound of the diamond grinding wheel, she performed the grinding and honing processes. She confirmed the improved sharpness by testing the knife on a tomato. She then sharpened other knives but gave up on sharpening scissors. Finally, she prepared a salad with onions and tomatoes during the stream, demonstrating the improved cutting ability of the sharpened knives, and concluding the broadcast with a sense of satisfaction.
