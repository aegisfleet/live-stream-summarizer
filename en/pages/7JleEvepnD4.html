📝 Overview

An offline collaboration between Suumiya Suu and Shiga Riko (Roromero). This first offline collaboration since their debut featured tasting the autumn delicacy "Tsukimi Burger" and playing the "Telepathy Game" to test their bond. During the talk, Suumiya Suu shared her impressions of eating her first-ever Tsukimi Burger, and surprising aspects of both members' personalities (such as their tolerance for gore and their sisterly dynamic) were revealed. While the Telepathy Game saw many unique answers, they ultimately announced the release of their second cover song, "Kitty"! They showcased a cool Roromero, different from their previous cover, "Nee Nee Nee."
