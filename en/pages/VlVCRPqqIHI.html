Overview

Usada Pekora streams her playthrough of the horror game 'Nippon Jiko Bukken Kanshi Kyoukai' (Japan Accident Property Monitoring Association). Pekora shows fear even before starting the game, which is based on the real-life task of monitoring stigmatized properties. Following the first property, she takes on monitoring T Shrine in Nara Prefecture, N Inn in Gifu Prefecture, and S Residence in Ibaraki Prefecture. At S Residence, various paranormal phenomena occur one after another, such as anomalies in tatami mat holes, creepy movements of a clown doll, automatic opening and closing of shutters, accelerated clocks, and repeatedly appearing human figures in the second-floor room. Pekora screams many times and continues to report, reducing omissions despite feeling confused and mentally exhausted. She eventually clears the regular monitoring tasks for all properties, feeling relieved by the sense of accomplishment after overcoming the frequent paranormal events. After clearing the game, she reads viewer comments and engages in various discussions, including the possibility of real properties and paranormal phenomena being utilized as a business, scientific explanations of sleep paralysis, and popular ramen topics. She shared her own terrifying experiences and thoughts, enjoying active interaction with viewers. The stream remained bright and lively with Pekora's characteristic talk throughout, allowing viewers to enjoy the lingering effects of the game.
