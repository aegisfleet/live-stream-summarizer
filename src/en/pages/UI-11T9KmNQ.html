A video depicting Hajime Todoroki, a one-panel shojo manga artist, being interviewed spontaneously without a script. Hajime's unique character settings are revealed one after another, such as being a precocious genius, a narcissist, and someone who can hear the voices of animals. She passionately talks about her works and creative process, but the content goes beyond the understanding of the interview staff, leading to comical exchanges. Towards the end, she unveils a famous scene from her representative work "Hajime 1/2," but the staff are puzzled by the abstract drawing, and Hajime even gets emotional. Overall, it's a humorous broadcast where her personality explodes.
