📝 Overview

This stream announced that Hakui Koyori is launching the 'Koeyuraku Project' in collaboration with JT Group's D-LAB. It is a long-term project aiming to realize a society with enriched minds through research related to voices, with plans for location-based segments and research outcome presentations. In the stream, in addition to project details, the results of a 'relaxation methods' survey collected from listeners were introduced. Koyori expressed empathy and opinions on various methods, including osteopathic manipulation/massage, listening to music, engaging in hobbies, home renovation, interacting with imaginary friends, and imagining the smiles of Holomem. The atmosphere remained harmonious throughout, building anticipation for future research activities.
