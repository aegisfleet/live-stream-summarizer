📝 Overview

VTuber Usada Pekora from Hololive played the demo version of "Militsioner," a game where she aims to escape from a city monitored by a giant policeman. While surprised by the strange world and the giant's presence, she attempted to escape by exploring the city, collecting items, and solving puzzles. Despite trying to escape while appeasing the policeman, she repeatedly drowned or was arrested. She received hints from a friend, discovered an underground passage and lock-picking tools, and explored routes for escape. She attempted to escape by opening a restaurant, sneaking past the policeman, and even using a cable car and train, but all attempts failed, leading to her being sent to prison. In the final part of this session, she tried to find an escape method while being baffled by the policeman's sleep-talking, but time ran out, and the game ended. The streamer discussed the game's absurd world and her expectations for the full version, thanking her viewers.
