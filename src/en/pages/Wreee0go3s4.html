Overview

Otonose Kanade's vertical singing stream. With the theme of "wanting to sing happily," she performed pre-selected songs and requested songs. She alternated between lighthearted talk and singing, maintaining a friendly atmosphere. From the middle of the stream onwards, she challenged high-difficulty songs such as "Luv-toki," "Overdose," "Uroko," "Gunjou Sanka," and "Tentai Kansoku," captivating viewers with her overwhelming vocal and expressive power. Especially during "Overdose," a unique exchange about the lyrics created excitement, and moments were felt where her vocal skill had grown. Towards the end, she performed "LAKI MODE," "Seiza ni Naretara," and "Cheerful Vibes Echo" for the first time, and also introduced her original songs, ReGLOSS cover songs, and LINE stickers. She concluded the stream with words of gratitude, making it a highly satisfying singing stream where a strong improvement in vocal skills and a sense of unity with the viewers were deeply felt.

Mood: Throughout, the atmosphere was bright and harmonious, with a very strong sense of unity between the streamer and the viewers. Especially during singing, there was a mixed atmosphere of exhilaration and emotion.
