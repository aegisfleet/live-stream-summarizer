This stream features Anya Melfissa playing "Bum Simulator." The player takes on the role of a homeless person, striving to reclaim their body, which was stolen by EvilWay Corp., with the help of their companion, an NPC in a shopping cart named "CARL." On their way to deliver pigeons to the Pigeon Lady, they are attacked by police officers and gangs but manage to reach her. The Pigeon Lady assigns a new mission: to procure materials from entertainers and rebuild the pigeon coop. Later, following Carl's instructions, they attempt the "Bug Raid" mission on an enemy base but fail. While collecting healing items and building materials on the street, they repeatedly engage in combat with taser-wielding police officers and enemy gangs. They encounter a blood-stained doctor who gives them the recipe for "Alcohol Alchemy" to concoct "FUS-DO-BEER." Using this, Anya completes a bizarre quest to throw wealthy people into trash cans and levels up. The narrative progresses to EvilWay Corp.'s laboratory, with comical developments continuing to entertain viewers through Anya's humorous reactions. In the final part of the stream, after finishing the gameplay, Anya announced future stream schedules and read Super Chats, expressing gratitude to her viewers.
