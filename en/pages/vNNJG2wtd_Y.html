📝 Overview

The latter part of a solo trip to Shodoshima, Kagawa Prefecture, focuses on the ferry journey at the end. The streamer, Akai Haato, engaged in a unique fantasy at a collaboration table with the popular anime 'Teasing Master Takagi-san', imagining herself sitting next to the protagonist, Nishikata, and acting like Takagi-san, but abandoned it halfway due to embarrassment. On the ferry's outdoor deck, she enjoyed the sea breeze and scenery, noticing that the ship's floor was olive-colored, a specialty of Shodoshima. She savored coffee milk and a tangerine from the school lunch set purchased at the movie village on the ferry back home, reflecting on her three-day trip and expressing her enthusiasm for the next one.
