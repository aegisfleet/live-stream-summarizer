📝 Overview

In "Hololive Horror 2025," hosted by Hololive member Shirakami Fubuki, participants tackle three major horror challenges. Following the VR horror game "DEVOUR" in VRChat, the "Canine Family" members—Fuwawa, Mococo, Ookami Mio, and Shirakami Fubuki—take on Japan's largest real-life horror attraction, "Senritsu Meikyu." The project aimed to test if members could sense the timing of ghosts through smell or intuition. Despite screaming at the relentless horrors within the labyrinth, the members successfully cooperated to escape. The stream offered a variety of reactions, from struggles with VR game controls to panic induced by real-life fear.

🎨 Mood: A horror experience where various emotions intertwined, from struggling with VR games and fear paralysis, to screaming and panic in a real haunted house, and finally, the relief of escape.
