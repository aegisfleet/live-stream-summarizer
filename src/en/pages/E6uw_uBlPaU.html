📝 Overview

This is a stream where Hololive's Yuzuki Choco plays the sequel to the horror game "Kinsokuchi - The Blue Demon's Cave -". Based on hints found in the previous stream, she grasps the Blue Demon's weaknesses and behavior patterns. This time, Komari, a doll possessed by a student's spirit, and the protagonist Himari, cooperate to protect the remaining children from the Blue Demon while attempting to escape. Despite frequently falling into desperate situations due to the repeated appearances of the Blue Demon and the difficulty of movement, they eventually devise a strategy utilizing the Blue Demon's characteristic "dislike of incense" and aim for a full clear. In this part, despite numerous game overs due to repeated appearances of the Blue Demon in the underground passage, they succeed in obtaining a crucial item called "Pearl" through teamwork. As the escape plan progresses, it's revealed that children were sacrificed in a past festival and that adults betrayed them, leading Komari to renew her determination to "survive together with everyone." They ultimately reach the normal ending, and as news reports a landslide in Ochiiwa Village with missing persons, Yuzuki Choco suggests that there are multiple endings and concludes the stream.

🎨 Mood: An atmosphere full of the tension and surprises unique to horror games, where viewers can enjoy the bonds between companions and the streamer's various reactions.
