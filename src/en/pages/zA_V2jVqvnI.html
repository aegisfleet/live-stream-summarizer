📝 Overview

A project where ReGLOSS's Hanamu Todokura plays the legendary freshman "Hajime Endai" of the prestigious "Todokura Music Academy" with unscripted improvisation. Hajime Endai, who entered the school as the top student, comically introduces the school's strict rules, a super-hard daily schedule, and traditional practice methods. He answers questions from the interview staff on the fly, sometimes making free-spirited remarks and actions that go beyond the setting, inviting laughter. Finally, he delivers an entrance ceremony speech and performs Todokura-style Momotaro, fully showcasing his charisma.

🎨 Mood: A comical and humorous atmosphere throughout, with Hanamu Todokura's improvisational acting being amusing and frequently eliciting laughter.
