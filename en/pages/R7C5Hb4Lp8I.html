Overview

This video is the music video for the song 'Dottabatta Chin Douchu!' by Hololive members Ookami Mio, Shirakami Fubuki, Oozora Subaru, and Sakura Miko. It's an enjoyable and heartwarming work that allows viewers to experience the feeling of traveling along with them.
