📝 Overview

Live stream of the horror game 'Japan Association for Monitoring Incident Properties'. This time, Polka performs surveillance duties at Akita Prefecture's A residence. As before, she identifies and reports anomalies from surveillance camera footage. This time, phenomena causing more direct fear, such as intruder invasions and ghost appearances, occur frequently, leading Polka to repeatedly react with surprise and terror. In particular, she reports the face of a ghost appearing on the lintel, earning a large special allowance. However, upon checking the 'reported anomalies' at Ibaraki Prefecture's S residence, it is revealed that many anomalies remain undiscovered, making her deeply realize the depth and difficulty of this game once again. Finally, a list of various anomalous phenomena accumulated from previous surveillance duties is displayed, drawing the player into even greater fear.

🎨 Mood: Throughout, it is enveloped in the tension and fear characteristic of a horror game, but Polka also shows confusion and frustration with the game's absurdity, and at times, humorous reactions.
