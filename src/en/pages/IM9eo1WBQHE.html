📝 Overview

【QQQbeats!!!】is a live stream where Shirakami Fubuki plays Taito's puzzle x rhythm game 'QQQbeats!!!' in a viewer-participatory format. During the stream, game rules, controls, and character introductions were explained. Shirakami Fubuki then played the story mode with Honami and Ame, charmed by their adorable characters and deep gameplay. Key features include 'Beat Attack,' which allows players to enter a rhythm game and obstruct opponents by matching 5 or more bubbles (bubbles disappear when 3 or more are matched), and using skills and abilities to gain an advantage in battles. Following this, she had two matches against viewers and then explained and played the new 'DUOps' mode twice. In DUOps mode, the goal is to defeat powerful enemies called 'Cheaters' through cooperative play, with strategies utilizing Fever and Special Hacks being crucial. The backstory of T-Company seeking player cooperation to combat Cheaters was also shared. Shirakami Fubuki enjoyed Hololive songs, secured victories with seamless teamwork with viewers and a guest (Mori Calliope's avatar), and concluded the stream with great excitement.
