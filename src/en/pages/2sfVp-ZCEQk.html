📝 Overview

This is a stream where Natsuiro Matsuri conducts the final business day of the Onigiri Shop Simulator. As the game's level approaches its cap and there's nothing left to do, the goal for this stream was to complete the game. She prepared onigiri (salmon, pickled plum, mentaiko, sesame salt, tenmusu, kombu) and side dishes (grilled onigiri, croquettes, horse mackerel fry, shrimp tempura), serving various customers who came to the shop. From the beginning to the middle, preparation work and a hectic customer rush continued, and in the latter half, customer visit frequency increased even further, requiring heightened concentration to avoid order mistakes. Pickled plum onigiri was very popular, with additional preparation quickly done after it sold out. During closing procedures, she managed inventory of remaining ingredients, products, and consumables, perfectly preparing for the next day's business. She enjoyed the game throughout, showing delight after purchasing a 'Maneki Neko' (lucky cat) and seeing its sales-boosting effect.

🎨 Mood: Generally peaceful throughout, with occasional hectic moments, but overall cheerful.
