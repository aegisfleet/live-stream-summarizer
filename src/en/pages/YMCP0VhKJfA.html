Overview

A stream where Tsunomaki Watame and Shishiro Botan work on constructing the "Coffee Cup" attraction in Minecraft's Hololive Summer Park. Despite struggling with Redstone circuits and minecart speed adjustments, they cooperate to experiment with physical layouts. They discover that setting up a Beacon changes the light color and are impressed by its magnificent light pillar. Unexpected animals like goats and creepers appear in large numbers around the attraction, adding to the liveliness. After completing the Coffee Cup base, they move on to building a water slide. For the water slide, they construct a system where minecarts move automatically by combining water flow, rails, and levers. After much trial and error, they successfully make the minecart move with water flow and proceed to improve it into an even longer slide. Towards the end of the stream, conversation with Kazama Iroha gets lively, and they try a test ride of the water slide she built. They successfully complete a safe ride and discuss the future development of the park and the conclusion of the stream.

Mood: An atmosphere filled with trial and error, discovery, and the joy of creation
