📝 Overview

A simulation game stream where Kirarara Vivi becomes the manager of a TCG card shop and runs her own store, 'Vivi Station'. Initially, she struggles with card display and sales, but strives to level up the shop by getting rare cards from pack openings and installing play tables for customers to enjoy card games in the store. There are also moments of struggle, such as cash shortages preventing inventory purchases and difficult discount negotiations with unique customers. However, through steady customer service and pricing strategies, the shop grows steadily. In the latter half, more expensive rare card transactions and unique interactions with customers (like repelling them with deodorant spray) occur, ultimately achieving stable management and sales. Vivi faced various challenges but successfully raised the shop level, completed over 100 transactions, and generated significant profit. Towards the end, she checked her shop's reviews and was delighted to see many positive ratings from customers. On the final day, after closing for the 7th day, the profit turned positive, and the store continued its steady growth. Vivi declared her intention to continue making the store thrive and concluded the stream.

🎨 Mood: A bright, lively atmosphere with Vivi's cheerful voice echoing throughout.
