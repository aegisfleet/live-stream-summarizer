📝 Overview

This is a stream where Otonose Kana plays the horror game 'Noroizuki'. In the early stages, she encounters an incident where the game progresses unintentionally, but she explores the school building and uncovers mysteries as the time limit approaches. She finds the pool key in the staff room and Akane's smartphone by the poolside, gaining new information about a 25-year-old incident. She discovers the relationship between Chizuko and the janitor Gon in the library, Yuji's body in the cooking room, and Chizuko's body in the incinerator, gradually revealing the truth of the incident. In the final phase, Akane, who had been missing, sends a LINE message, bringing the story to a new turning point.

🎨 Mood: A somewhat tense atmosphere, but eased by troubles and Kana's humorous reactions. The tension increases towards the end.
