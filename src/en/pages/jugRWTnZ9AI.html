📝 Overview

This is the final part of Korone Inugami's impromptu Among Us stream with Hololive members. Set on the new map 'The Fungle,' the members are bewildered by new roles and tasks, but the deduction and psychological warfare among them intensify. In the heated discussions, Engineer Chihaya, Map Monitor Kanade, and Tensaisuu are successively ejected, but all of them turn out to be Crewmates, making it extremely difficult to identify the Imposter. Ultimately, the Imposter wins with an an unexpected strategic victory, and the stream concludes with a vow for revenge on the Polus map next time.
