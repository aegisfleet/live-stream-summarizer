📝 Overview

A special project featuring Usada Pekora and Inugami Korone titled 'Does Usada Pekora Have Hololive Love? Quiz'. Pekora appeared in a prisoner's uniform and was teased that she was in a room for people who don't have Hololive love. The quiz consisted of 20 questions, with 15 or more points required to be certified as having Hololive love. Despite Pekora's efforts, she only answered 13 questions correctly, narrowly missing the certification standard. However, there were many instances where she demonstrated her Hololive love at key points, such as questions about YAGOO's real name and her own live performances. At the end of the stream, an announcement for Pekora's new costume reveal stream and a promise for a 'bath collaboration' with Inugami Korone also emerged, bringing the stream to a lively and amicable close.
