📝 Overview

This is a live stream where Hololive's Usada Pekora and Takane Lui present a pre-release play-through of the prologue for Tookyo Games' new title "Shuuten Kyoudan" (The Doomsday Cult), led by Kazutaka Kodaka. Set in the apocalyptic 'Shuuten Theocracy,' the story unfolds with a mysterious plot where the protagonist, having lost their memory, learns that they are dead and also the cult's founder. In the time-limited 'Trial of God,' they are tasked with identifying and making the culprit confess to their murder. During this process, 'reincarnation' through 'God's power' is revealed. A system where player choices branch into five different genres is introduced, and high expectations for the game are shared along with final announcements for its release date and platforms.
