Overview

Omaru Polka held her first morning stream as she entered her 6th year of activities. She enjoyed interacting with her listeners and talked about participating in Comiket and her 5th-anniversary merchandise. In particular, she enthusiastically delved into the horror anime "The Summer Hikaru Died" and the interactive content "The 4th Boundary," which she's recently been hooked on, passionately explaining their appeal, horror, and the creators' dedication. She also spoke passionately about her deep love and enthusiasm for entertainment, including her emotional experience at the Aikatsu live performance with a live band, her inclination towards the underground idol scene, and her eagerness for a karaoke stream with Hololive members. She detailed her 5th-anniversary merchandise (acrylic panel, can badges, hand-holding plushies, bonus card), passionately discussing their designs and gimmicks. Furthermore, she expressed her anticipation for the upcoming major event "Nepolabo Live," the bond with her listeners, and gratitude for her daily activities. While showing care for listeners enjoying their summer vacation and those heading to work, she demonstrated her commitment to taking on various challenges in her 6th year of activities.

Mood: The atmosphere was consistently cheerful and amicable, emphasizing interaction with listeners.
