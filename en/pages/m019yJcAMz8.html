📝 Overview

The 'Alcohol Draft Meeting 2025' was held as a special project for Omaru Polka's show, 'Polka no Densetsu' (Polka's Legend). Following the highly popular previous 'Snack Draft,' Polka and the program staff (Izutsu) competed in a draft format to select 'alcoholic beverages' suitable for pre-selected snacks. Polka was surprised by the surprisingly strong lineup of snacks prepared by the staff (Izutsu) but countered using her knowledge of alcohol. Izutsu ultimately won overwhelmingly, leading to a series of comical exchanges, even though Polka felt frustrated.
