📝 Overview

Due to the gathering of "Pon" (clumsy/airheaded) players, basic game rules like responding to sabotages were often neglected, leading to a continuous streak of victories for the Impostors. The Crewmates maintained a chaotic state, often mistakenly ejecting fellow Crewmates during discussions. In the mid-to-late stages, coordination started to work, leading to some victories via Impostor ejection and task completion. In the final phase, "Infected Mode (Zombie)" was introduced, resulting in a mix of Crewmate task victories and swift Infected victories, closing the stream with a blend of chaos and excitement.
