📝 Overview

Hololive's Natsuiro Matsuri takes on "Onigiri Shop Simulator." As an onigiri shop owner, she experiences the entire process: cooking rice, making onigiri, wrapping them in seaweed, bagging them, and serving customers. Her shop level successfully rises, and she starts selling mentaiko onigiri! Her struggle continues as she devises ways to improve efficiency, such as arranging ingredients and attempting mass production. The long-awaited grilled onigiri becomes available, but she struggles with the difficulty of perfecting the grilling. Despite her ongoing confusion with croquette orders, she skillfully serves customers and steadily increases profits. The highlight is Matsuri's growth as she enjoys running the onigiri shop, even amidst the busyness of one-person operations.

🎨 Mood: Cheerful and growing despite challenges
