Day 1 of Hololive's official 3-night consecutive project 'Natsu-Kyun'. La+ Darknesss produced Kazama Iroha, creating two summer-themed 'heart-fluttering movies'. La+'s unique directorial guidance and Kazama's earnest performance despite her bewilderment, along with the comical interactions in the behind-the-scenes footage, deliver both 'flutter' and 'laughter' to the viewers.
