📝 Overview

This is a continuation of the stream where hololive English's Bijou and Advent members (Shiori, Nerissa, FuwaMoco) play the horror co-op game 'DARKWATER'. The game progresses with exploration from a submarine, but they are plagued by frequent game crashes and communication issues. Bijou experiences an accident where she is eaten by a giant creature while acting as a mouse, leading to game restarts each time. Afterward, at the suggestion of the members, a soothing session is also held where they discuss their ideal jobs. In the latter half, a unique ASMR-like sound battle unfolds, along with a free talk filled with unique episodes from the members regarding bone fracture experiences and tooth-brushing habits, maintaining a chaotic yet fun atmosphere. Towards the end, discussions were held about the cause of the game crashes, and announcements were made regarding future activities, such as new songs from Advent members, Nerissa's parent-child collaboration, and Shiori's stream of another game, concluding the broadcast with gratitude to the viewers.

🎨 Mood: A chaotic atmosphere, constantly tossed around by game troubles, yet filled with the members' unique ideas and endless laughter. While at times healing and at times strange remarks fly around, a consistently warm and friendly mood prevails.
