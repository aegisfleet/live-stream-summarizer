📝 Overview

This is an introductory video for Hololive's new voice content "Kimi to Tsunagaru, Denwa♥Voice" (Connecting with You, Phone♥Voice). In this content, two characters, Kira Vivi and Suimiya Kaname, talk to the listener over the phone. In the video, Vivi encourages the listener's work, but then reveals her true feelings that she was actually lonely and wanted to hear their voice, leading to a sweet invitation for a shopping date. Suimiya Kaname, while a little nervous, confesses that she was thinking about the listener, offering a sweet and gentle phone date that provides healing and comfort.
