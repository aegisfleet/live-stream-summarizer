Streamer Zeta plays the survival game "BUM Simulator". After completing the previous begging mission, she heads to Bugs to retrieve her stolen pigeon. After confronting Bugs, Zeta follows Carl's instructions to give Bugs the middle finger and then defeat him with a "pee attack". Carl grants Zeta a new pigeon skill called "Shuriken Pigeon" and encourages her to strengthen her pigeon with the help of Pigeon Lady. Upon arriving at Pigeon Lady's shop, Zeta learns that rebuilding the pigeon coop is her next goal. However, she soon fails an extreme mission from the "Entertainer" Richman and is chased by the police. Afterwards, Mr. Jello tasks her with a timed bomb delivery mission, which she also fails. Mr. Jello installs a recycling box and provides materials for repairing the pigeon coop. Zeta helps a bullied Creep in a new mission and earns Respect points after fighting the Bugs gang. She uses the money earned from begging to buy ice cream, but it turns out to be an unconsumable throwing item. Searching for a drink, she finds and drinks expired milk from a trash can. She continues to collect materials, but as the stream is about to end, an attack on her base by the Bugs gang is announced, prompting Zeta to hastily end the stream.
