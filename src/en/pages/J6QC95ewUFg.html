📝 Overview

This stream marks day 22 of Sakura Miko's endurance project to catch a shiny golden Magikarp in Pokémon Ruby. Due to a break since the last session, Miko struggled at first to recall the game's controls and her progress, but soon engaged in battles with Magikarp. The middle part of the stream transitioned into a lively chat session filled with summer memories and anecdotes involving Hololive members. Amusing stories were shared, such as losing her phone during the Harry Potter Studio Tour, Mio-chan's serious acting, and episodes with teachers from her school days. There was also a discussion about the retro boom and personal values, maintaining a friendly atmosphere throughout as Miko interacted with her viewers. The stream concluded with nostalgic topics, looking back at past broadcasts. The conversation shifted from generational theories to Chiikawa and Ichiran Ramen, finally culminating in her passionate talk about Chiikawa Cafe merchandise.
