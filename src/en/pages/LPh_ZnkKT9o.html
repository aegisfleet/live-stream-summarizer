📝 Overview

This is a live stream where three Hololive members, Inugami Korone, Shirogane Noel, and Himemori Luna, introduce a collaboration campaign between Coca-Cola and Star Wars. The three, dressed in Star Wars costumes, talked about campaign merchandise, AR experiences, and special sales venues, highlighting key points. In the middle, they had an exciting segment where they compared Star Wars characters to Hololive members. In the latter half, a viewer-participatory present campaign quiz was held. Although the quiz questions were announced, time ran out, and the answers were postponed until the next session. Fans were encouraged to enjoy the stream with a Coca-Cola in hand, and efforts were made to ensure that even those unfamiliar with Star Wars could enjoy it.
