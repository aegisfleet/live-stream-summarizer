This is a continuation of Nerissa's collaboration stream with her Hololive English colleagues playing 'Darkwater'. Ultimately, only Nerissa survives and manages to reunite with her teammates, but they remain constantly on the brink of crisis. Between gameplay segments, the stream also features relaxed chatter, humorously recounting in-stream troubles, discussing ideal professions, and planning a Christmas party. This stream is captivating with its horror game-specific tension and the lively interactions of the players.
