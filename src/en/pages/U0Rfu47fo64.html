Overview

A 3D collaboration stream by hololive EN's JustICE members. It started with the unveiling of the members' 3D models and a live performance of their original song 'ABOVE BELOW'. They then took on various games prepared by their manager, Smoky. In 'Speed Block,' a cooperative block-stacking game, there was an incident where the bomb exploded due to a time limit. In the latter half, Raora won 'Hammer of Justice! Whack-a-Gnome!' after a fierce gnome-whacking struggle. Following this, three original songs, 'Renegade,' 'BLUE CLAPPER,' and 'Breaking Dimensions,' were performed in a 3D live. The stream concluded with the announcement of new merchandise and a performance at the hololive EN 3rd Concert, ending a broadcast filled with member camaraderie and energy.

Mood: A consistently friendly atmosphere, characterized by strong teamwork and lively banter among the members. It included emotional performances, fun segments, and announcements that built anticipation for the future.
