📝 Overview

A collaboration stream of the deep-sea survival horror game 'DARKWATER' by Hololive Advent members FUWAMOCO, Shiori Novella, Koseki Bijou, and Nerissa Ravencroft. Playing for the first time and figuring out the rules as they went, they faced the eerie atmosphere of the deep sea with their characteristic cheerfulness, despite trembling with fear. Nerissa's PC audio issues and game crashes provided laughs while the game progressed. Ultimately, everyone was eliminated and it was game over, but they showed enthusiasm for a retry. In the latter half's chat, topics like whether or not to eat kiwi skin and game bugs on Windows 11 were lively discussed. Various announcements were also made, including Advent's original songs, Nerissa's 'Father's' debut stream, and Koseki Bijou's acoustic live. Finally, FUWAMOCO announced a continuous stream of 'Silent Hill F' and expressed gratitude to the viewers, ending the stream on a lively note.
