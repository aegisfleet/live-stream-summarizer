📝 Overview

This is the second half of the "Challenge to La+ Darknesss" project hosted by Hakui Koyori of hololive. La+ Darknesss and Houshou Marine challenge a quiz to guess Hakui Koyori's answers. Unique questions such as "What would you do if the world ended?", "What if your YouTube channel got banned?", and "What if La+ said 'Welcome home, darling. Dinner or me?'" were thrown out, leading to hilarious and unexpected answers and results throughout. Ultimately, La+ did not achieve a perfect score, but a special announcement was made: the release of a cover song video "Ojisan Koubun" by La+ Darknesss, Hakui Koyori, and Houshou Marine.
