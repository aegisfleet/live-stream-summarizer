A collaboration stream of the cooperative mining simulation game 'Keep Digging' featuring 5 members: Otonose Kanade, Shirakami Fubuki, Natsuiro Matsuri, Yukihana Lamy, and Hoshimachi Suisei. After initial upgrades, they advanced through checkpoints and ancient ruins deep underground (400m, 700m). Along the way, they discovered a golden chimpanzee statue (Osa) and underground mining facilities, while mining large quantities of high-value minerals like huge ores (Deca ores) and Adamantite. They successfully activated a giant machine located 1000m underground, achieving game completion.
