From Nekomata Okayu's perspective, a Minecraft Werewolf RPG featuring Hololive members was held. The members, gathered under the guise of a summer forest stroll tour, were met with the shocking revelation that they would engage in a deathmatch at the Werewolf Mansion. The first game saw a clear victory for the Werewolf team. In the second match, new roles "Wolf-Possessed" and "Baker" were added, further increasing the game's complexity. Despite Nekomata Okayu being on the Werewolf side, the Villager team achieved a splendid victory. In the subsequent third match, Nekomata Okayu was a Villager, but the Werewolf team won. The fourth match ended with the Vampire team's victory, pushing the final decision to the fifth match. The Werewolf game, unfolding amidst suspicion and distrust, consistently creates different dramas.
