Overview

Hololive EN's IRyS takes on the online cooperative game 'PEAK' (an Only Up!-style climbing game) with HAACHAMA and Shiori Novella. Despite repeated attempts, mishaps occur from the very beginning. Starting with survival after a plane crash, the stream features unpredictable incidents such as IRyS turning into a ghost, teammates repeatedly falling to their deaths due to accidental Chain Launcher shots, and succumbing to poison areas. There are also moments of enjoying character customization in new stages like 'SHORE' and 'MESA', and efforts to overcome obstacles collaboratively. It's a non-stop, captivating survival climbing stream filled with a mix of laughter and despair. Ultimately, everyone succumbs in a desert stage ravaged by fire and poison, marking the end of this challenge.

Mood: Consistently amiable, with numerous mishaps and comical moments leading to endless laughter. However, due to the game's difficulty and unexpected developments, there are occasional moments of anxiety and despair.
