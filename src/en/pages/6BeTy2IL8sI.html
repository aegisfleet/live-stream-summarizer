Overview

Roboco from hololive and Higuchi Kaede from Nijisanji held a long-awaited reunion collaboration stream. They appeared in angel and devil uniforms and announced the premiere of their cover song video with a heaven and hell theme. During the stream, they played games in Clubhouse Games: 51 Worldwide Classics until the premiere time of the announcement video, engaging in intense battles of Othello and Hit & Blow. Especially in Hit & Blow, their guesses went back and forth, with Higuchi Kaede ultimately winning. They attempted Yacht as the final game, but time ran out and the game ended. Throughout the stream, viewers enjoyed the intimate conversation between the two and their lively reactions during the games.

Mood: A consistently amicable atmosphere, with intimate conversations unique to two people who know each other well, and lively reactions during games, making it an enjoyable stream.
