📝 Overview

Rindo Chihaya streamed an early access play of the full release version of "Shutoko Battle". In the middle of the stream, she unlocked the PERK tree and adjusted game settings, then challenged SP Battles with a normal Civic. However, she was no match for high-level rivals and lost. Later, she switched to her beloved RX-8, interacted with rivals at Hakozaki PA, and went on to win SP Battles, leading to an intense race. The biggest announcement was an official collaboration: two original songs by Rindo Chihaya will be implemented in the game, and her dedicated custom car will also appear. The game will be fully released on September 25th and will also be exhibited at the Tokyo Game Show.

🎨 Mood: Excited throughout, showing deep love and anticipation for the game. Especially when the collaboration was announced, her joy exploded.
