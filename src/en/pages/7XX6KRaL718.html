📝 Overview

Natsuiro Matsuri of Hololive, along with Shirakami Fubuki and Omaru Polka, challenged a solo parallel run project for "R.E.P.O.". They competed to see how far they could progress within a 2-hour time limit by collecting in-game items and defeating enemies. The early stages began with comical developments, repeatedly rerolling for the desired map. Gameplay was a mix of thrill and anxiety, including battles with Gnomes in Headman Manor and encountering a large number of enemies at the Service Station. Towards the middle, despite struggling with new enemies "Robe" and "Trudge", she steadily collected items and upgraded, eventually defeating the formidable "Metalist". In the final "Magic Academy" section, she efficiently collected high-value items and invincible drones, rapidly earning funds. In the remaining short time, she successfully made the final delivery and splendidly reached Level 8. The project ultimately concluded with Natsuiro Matsuri's victory.

🎨 Mood: From a comical and lively atmosphere in the beginning, it shifts to a mix of thrill and anxiety after the game starts.
