📝 Overview

As the final day of their Hokkaido trip, the ReGLOSS members visited 'AOAO SAPPORO Aquarium' in Sapporo. They were soothed by the beautiful tanks of nature aquariums, the rainforest exhibits, and the adorable penguins. For breakfast, they enjoyed croissants at the aquarium's bakery, then explored Nijo Market, encountering fresh seafood and local specialties like melons unique to Hokkaido. For their last meal in Hokkaido, they savored their chosen kaisendon (seafood bowls) and crab soup at the luxurious kaisendon specialty restaurant 'Oiso'. They looked back on their three-day Hokkaido trip, having made many memories.

🎨 Mood: Throughout, the atmosphere was harmonious, conveying how the members thoroughly enjoyed Hokkaido's beautiful nature and delicious cuisine, sharing their emotions and excitement.
