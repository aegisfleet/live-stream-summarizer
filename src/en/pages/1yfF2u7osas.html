📝 Overview

A long-awaited singing stream by Ookami Mio. Following a selection of songs themed around farewells, she performed "Aitsura Zenin Dousoukai" by ZUTOMAYO (Zutto Mayonaka de Ii no ni.) to regain her energy. Furthermore, during the stream, the decision was suddenly made to sing Nekomata Okayu's song "Dotabata Chinchoudou," which was made possible by the incredible support of her manager. She concluded by emotionally singing "Yasashii Suisei" by YOASOBI. After the stream, she also announced the premiere of her original song's music video and an evening collaboration project.

🎨 Mood: Full of emotion and determination, with a touch of playfulness at times
