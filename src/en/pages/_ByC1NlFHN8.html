📝 Overview

A collaborative stream where four members, Aki Rosenthal, Shiranui Flare, Yuzuki Choco, and Amane Kanata, play the updated 'REPOST'. They aim to escape by collecting items on a vast map. Due to unpredictable enemy onslaughts, laser traps, and mysterious old man robots, members are repeatedly defeated, turning into severed heads. Flare then collects these heads to revive her teammates, leading to an extraordinary development. Despite numerous crises, Flare's astonishing performance, Aki Rosenthal's precise instructions, and Choco-sensei and Kanata's cheerful reactions keep the game moving forward. Ultimately, time runs out, and despite a mishap regarding saving methods, they promise to resume in the next collaboration, concluding the stream in a harmonious atmosphere.
