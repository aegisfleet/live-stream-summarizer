📝 Overview

Shirakami Fubuki played Day 9 and Day 10 of the surveillance horror game 'No, I'm not a Human'. Facing difficult choices daily, Day 9 accelerated the story with the identification of a neighboring couple as Visitors, the difficult decision that followed, and the appearance of a fanatical cult. Day 10 finally introduced a phase where the protagonist must inspect themselves, leading the player to confront doubts about their own humanity. Ultimately, the protagonist mistakenly killed a visitor (a man with his family) who was trying to prove he was human, leading to a restart. After that, they encountered FEMA agents and armed Visitor hunters, and their non-cooperative attitude resulted in a gruesome 'Genocide End', concluding the stream.
