Overview

This is a live playthrough of the horror game "DREAD FLATS Kyōgū". The protagonist, staying in an abandoned apartment building for a high reward, uncovers the mystery of a mass disappearance amidst an eerie atmosphere. As dreams and reality intertwine, they are driven to the brink by the sound of beads, unsettling silhouettes, and blood-written messages, falling into an endless loop of recurring terrifying experiences. The streamer, Otonose Kanade, entertains viewers with shouts of surprise and terror, and occasional humorous comments, even as she is frightened by the continuous appearance of strange phenomena and enemy characters. The game progresses with a creeping dread that wears down the player's mind, featuring unreasonable developments such as enemies discovering the player even when hidden, and unexpected events like enemies appearing while adjusting the volume. Ultimately, the protagonist discovers a key item to escape the loop, sacrifices their own body to overcome the nightmare, and returns to the real world. However, the truth behind the incident and the protagonist's subsequent mental state remain a mystery, and the game concludes hinting at the existence of new DLC. Otonose Kanade stated that this was the scariest game of the year, making for a stream filled with both fear and a sense of accomplishment.
