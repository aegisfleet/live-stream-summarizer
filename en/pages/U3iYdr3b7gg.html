A collaborative 3D Among Us stream featuring 10 Hololive members. The stream host, Omaru Polka, organized a "Gachi-Pon" (Seriously Clumsy) session, gathering members who are notoriously bad at Among Us. However, the session started dramatically when Polka herself became the Impostor in the first game and performed exceptionally well. The atmosphere remained lively and filled with laughter throughout, featuring intense (?) battles between the clumsy Crewmates and the Impostors. In the latter half, the clumsiness accelerated, and as Crewmates were eliminated one after another, a major Impostor sabotage succeeded, leading to the unexpected defeat and total elimination of the Crewmates. Confused deductions by the clumsy Crewmates continued, resulting in a chaotic flow. In the final phase, there was an exchange of kills and sabotages, with the Crew repeatedly failing to resolve sabotages. Ultimately, the Impostors, La+ Darknesss and a mysterious Red player, claimed victory. In the final game mode, "CRITICAL CARGO," the Crewmates won by completing their tasks, but the subsequent game ended dramatically when the Infected side (Zombies) rapidly infected all Crewmates to secure a victory.
