📝 Overview

Announcement trailer for hololive's new voice pack project, "Tonari no Seki Voice" (Voice from the Seat Next to You). Sample voices by Natsuiro Matsuri (Daytime/Classroom window seat) and Shiori Novella (Nighttime/Train seat, English audio included) are introduced in contrasting scenarios. The voice content consists of friendly greetings and invitations directed at the person sitting next to them. Finally, a list of 25 participating talents from hololive, hololive Indonesia, hololive English, and hololive DEV_IS is revealed, announcing the start of sales on October 9, 2025.
