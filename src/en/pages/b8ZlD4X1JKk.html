📝 Overview

This stream features Gigi playing the viewer-participation puzzle game "Is this seat taken?". Gigi aimed to clear each stage by assigning characters with various requests to seats in diverse locations, trying unique solutions. Despite struggling to fulfill numerous (and sometimes contradictory) demands, she eventually perfectly cleared all stages, unlocking new cities "Brussels" and "London". After the game, she shared her thoughts, thanked viewers, and announced upcoming collaborations with "Ninomae Ina'nis" and participation in an anime event with "Pavolia Reine", concluding the stream in a warm atmosphere.
