📝 Overview

Continuation of the card shop management simulation. The business expands smoothly by increasing funds through buying and selling high-value cards and opening packs. In the middle section, the streamer experimented with hiring part-timers and setting up match rules. Towards the end, more high-value rare cards were pulled. Furthermore, countermeasures against the "body odor problem" were implemented, including the introduction of an automatic deodorizer (50,000 yen), focusing on improving the store environment. Although the shop ultimately reached Level 15, the financial settlement on Day 18 showed a deficit due to mounting expenses such as labor costs and renovation fees. Facing a difficult business situation, the streamer restocked inventory, adjusted card selling prices, and proceeded to the next day's operation. The stream concluded upon preparation for opening on the morning of Day 19.

🎨 Mood: Experimenting and rejoicing over the appearance of expensive cards, creating a lively and fun atmosphere overall, which then shifts abruptly towards the end when facing financial difficulties
