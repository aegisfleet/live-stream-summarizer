📝 Overview

Sakura Miko takes on the free horror game "Kyoufu no Saitan -Score the Fear-". Towards the end, Miko intentionally continues giving high scores to collect the lenient ending (D) and the ending (E) where mid-level scores trigger the recurrence of memory. Especially in Ending E, it is revealed that the protagonist (the older brother) unconsciously sealed away the memory of his younger brother's (Yuu-kun) death. The game shifts from a horror experience to a deeply moving and sad story of brotherhood. Miko, deeply touched by the profound story, successfully collected all endings and completed the game.
