📝 Overview

This is the second half of Tokino Sora's 8th-anniversary countdown stream. Details for the 8th-anniversary live concert 'Starlight Bloom' were announced, including daily changing setlists and streaming tickets. She touched upon her extensive discography and expressed her ambition to reach 100 songs by her 10th anniversary. Additionally, she revealed personal background details, such as majoring in vocal music at a junior college, her past shyness in front of people, and a period when she aimed for behind-the-scenes work out of admiration for idols. Expressing deep gratitude to her fans, she vowed to create an infinite future together with them in her 9th year, through activities that fully embrace her 'likes,' such as ASMR readings and Final Fantasy playthroughs.
