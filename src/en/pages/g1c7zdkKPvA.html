📝 Overview

Rindo Chihaya held a vertical karaoke stream, a broadcast to soothe the fatigue of Friday. Aiming for 500,000 subscribers, she challenged a vertical format stream. She successively performed popular songs such as 'Bunny Girl,' 'Sugar Song and Bitter Step,' 'Takaneno Hanakosan,' 'Ikenai Taiyou,' 'Zankyo Sanka,' 'HANABI,' 'Bibideba,' 'Himawari no Yakusoku,' 'Yume wo Kanaete Doraemon,' 'Shoujo Rei,' 'Kutsu no Hanabi,' 'Cinderella Boy,' and 'Kimi no Shiranai Monogatari.' Towards the end of the stream, while revealing she was hungry, she reaffirmed her policy of 'singing songs that make everyone feel energized without setting a specific theme,' through interaction with fans. She delivered her singing voice in a consistently friendly and energetic atmosphere. She also announced her participation in Hololive's game project '7 Days to Die' and called for channel subscriptions as she approaches her 1st anniversary.
