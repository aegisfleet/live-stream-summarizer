📝 Overview

This is a stream where Hololive's Omaru Polka, along with Shirakami Fubuki and Roboco, play "PEAK," a Mario Maker-style climbing game. The three explore a vast map, collecting items and cooperating to reach the summit. Along the way, they struggle with difficult terrains and mechanics such as cactus fields, narrow ravines, and lava zones, encountering numerous mishaps while sometimes encouraging each other and sometimes teasing one another. Particularly in the latter half of the caldera's lava zone, there was a desperate situation where they were completely wiped out, but they continued to challenge without giving up and eventually cleared the game. At the end of the stream, while mentioning several unachieved accomplishments, they showed enthusiasm for another attempt, expressed gratitude to the viewers and members, and concluded the stream with smiles.
