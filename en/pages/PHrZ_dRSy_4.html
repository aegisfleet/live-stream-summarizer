📝 Overview

A 3D live concert commemorating the release of Takane Lui's 2nd album 'Lapis Lazuli'. Hololive members (Hakos Baelz, Shirakami Fubuki, Momosuzu Nene, Tsunomaki Watame, Hoshimachi Suisei) were welcomed as guests, performing songs from the album and collaboration songs with the guests. In the middle of the live, a major announcement about the album was made, and after the collaboration song 'ZIG-ZAG' with Hoshimachi Suisei was performed, the music video for the new song 'DARE!?' was released and the song was performed for the first time. The live performance was lively and full of passion from beginning to end.
