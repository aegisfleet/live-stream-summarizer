📝 Overview

A recap stream of Usada Pekora's 6th Anniversary Live 'Pekora RUSH!!'. Pekora expresses gratitude to viewers while thoroughly discussing behind-the-scenes details of the live performance, including the opening video, reasons for song selections, and anecdotes with co-stars. The stream progresses in a consistently friendly atmosphere, as Pekora explains the live's concept, which heavily featured pachislot-style productions, her feelings embedded in each song, and particular staging details. In this broadcast, Pekora primarily revealed the reasons for choosing guest members, secret stories behind song selections, and specific stage directions for six songs: 'Long shot,' 'Nijiiro no Flügel,' 'Kokoro yo Genshi ni Modore,' 'Goodbye Sengen,' 'SANADANCE,' and 'I'm Pekora.' Additionally, 6th-anniversary merchandise was introduced.
