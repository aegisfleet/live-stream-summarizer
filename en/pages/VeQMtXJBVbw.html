📝 Overview

A livestream where VTuber "Kogane Hieko" takes on "Onigiri Shop Simulator." In the early stages, she encounters mishaps such as screen troubles, damaged cooking utensils, and stolen ingredients. Despite choosing the "Very Difficult" setting, she steadily manages the shop thereafter. Facing food loss issues, she introduces firm onigiri and a new menu item, umeboshi onigiri. Sales grow steadily, achieving shop level-up and unlocking mentaiko onigiri. However, in the final stages, new challenges emerge: she's overwhelmed by a surge of customers, faces ingredient shortages, cooking mistakes, and even the manager's customer service attitude deteriorates. Ultimately, while securing a profit, the stream concludes with an ending that suggests a continuation, leaving the manager's emotional behavior and food loss solutions unresolved.

🎨 Mood: The early stage starts with a peaceful atmosphere, where she enjoys herself despite being confused by mishaps. From the middle stage, she faces the difficulties of management and food loss, showing serious expressions. In the final stage, anxiety and fatigue accumulate from dealing with the rush of customers, and there are many scenes where the manager's attitude becomes cold or emotional.
