Overview

After FUWAMOCO's 3D models were unveiled, they played "HOLOLIVE GOROGORO MOUNTAIN," a game where they roll Hololive members in deformed ball forms to reach the goal. After setting a good record on the Tokino Sora stage, they successively cleared new stages: "Return of the Houshou Pirate Island" Stage 3, "Polka Show!", and "This Is Work Daily Life Edition". They also cleared "FUWAMOCO Island!" and "Holo Graffiti" stages, then challenged the final stage, "hololive Fes Final Stage," but ran out of time and got a game over. In each stage, Holomem balls with different appearances emerged, and they enjoyed playing with cute reactions and occasional mishaps.

Mood: A consistently peaceful and cheerful atmosphere, occasionally with moments of panic due to mishaps, and a touch of frustration towards the end.
