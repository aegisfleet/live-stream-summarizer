Overview

This is a stream where Natsuiro Matsuri from Hololive challenges the "Nightmare" class in Shadowverse Worlds Beyond. Although she experienced a series of losses early on, she deepened her understanding of the game with advice from viewers, achieving her first victory and showing growth. However, in the latter half of the stream, she struggled against the "Nemesis" class's powerful board-clearing "Puppet" deck, leading to more consecutive losses. Afterward, she renewed her determination and challenged a Bishop in the final match, but unfortunately, the stream ended before a conclusion could be reached, postponing the decision to the next time. Despite jokingly saying "Stop, stop, this is boring!" and retiring during defeats, her consistently bright and positive attitude while enjoying the game made for a memorable stream.

Mood: A consistently amicable atmosphere, with serious expressions seen during gameplay, yet a bright stream where she maintained her enjoyment even when losing.
