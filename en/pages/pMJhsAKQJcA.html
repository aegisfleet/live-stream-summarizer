Overview

Shirakami Fubuki held a chat stream discussing the appeal of the ramen chain 'Ichiran'. She invited viewers to share their recommended ways to eat Ichiran and their experiences at its stores, then showcased them with photos, delving into Ichiran's unique characteristics and charms. A wide range of topics were covered, including Ichiran's unique flavor concentration booths, its noodle refill system (kaedama), noodle manufacturing methods, and its secret sauce. Towards the end, the discussion also touched upon Ichiran's overseas expansion and factory tours, reaffirming its profound depth. The stream concluded with a lively discussion about eating Ichiran with other HoloMembers, described by Fubuki as 'just a personal fetish,' leading into the official animation's ending.
