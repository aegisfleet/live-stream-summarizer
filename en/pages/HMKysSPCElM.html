Overview

Akai Haato from Hololive hosts an "Occult Talk" stream, reading scary stories submitted by viewers. She shares occult experiences not usually discussed in her regular streams, such as introductions to her birthday merchandise and behind-the-scenes stories from her birthday shoot. She listens to ghost stories from her audience, reacting to a wide range of frightening tales including her grandfather's mysterious experience, a childhood horror experience at a shopping mall, and the truth behind a friend's death, sharing a unique late-night atmosphere. In the latter half, she delves into philosophical considerations about the weight and location of the soul, and discusses the importance of "memory" in the humanity of demons from the popular anime 'Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba'. Furthermore, she recounts unsettling insect-related experiences, citing the proverb "Even a worm has a soul," a strange SNS account anomaly that felt like real-life stalking, and inexplicable events she personally experienced during sleep paralysis. Through deep discussion and sharing with her listeners, the stream became a blend of curiosity and fear.

Mood: A stream where curiosity and fear intertwine, with chilling ghost stories interspersed throughout a consistently peaceful atmosphere.
