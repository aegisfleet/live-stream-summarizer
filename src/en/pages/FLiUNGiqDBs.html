📝 Overview

This is a stream where Yuzuki Choco plays the horror game 'Noroizuki'. The protagonist discovers a video of an incinerator projected in the 3rd-floor science room and learns about an incident that occurred at the incinerator 25 years ago. They succeed in finding Chizuko's body in the incinerator and breaking the curse, but a new cursed video is sent from Akane's smartphone. Later, the protagonist finds a video in the incinerator of Gonda throwing Chizuko into it and breaks that curse. Finally, they discover Akane's body in the pool, use a firecracker to make the ghost disappear, and the game reaches a good ending.
