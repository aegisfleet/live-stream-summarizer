📝 Overview

Four members, Shirakami Fubuki, Omaru Polka, Ichijou Ririka, and Otonose Kanade, played a mountain climbing game called 'PEAK', aiming for the summit collaboratively. After an emergency landing on a tropical coast due to a crash, they navigated challenging paths, utilizing various mechanisms, items, and unique characters. They struggled with poison and traps in the tropical area, leading to a game over for everyone, but they re-challenged in 'Beginner Mode'. Even in Beginner Mode, they encountered obstacles like 'Popopo' and incidents such as members becoming transparent due to audio bugs. They also attempted to open new routes with anti-gravity ropes but found it difficult. Ultimately, they got another game over, but were revived by the power of an ancient statue and reached 'High Mountain Day 6'. They ended the stream by roasting marshmallows, looking forward to the next adventure.
