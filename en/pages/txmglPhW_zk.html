📝 Overview

A collaborative livestream of the horror game "Dread Flats / Inauspicious Abode" (凶寓) by Shirakami Fubuki and Oogami Mio. Set in a dilapidated apartment building, the game involves exploring, searching for items, and solving puzzles to uncover the mystery of paranormal phenomena. The blurred lines between dream and reality, coupled with sudden appearances of eerie entities, lead to constant screams and shouts, offering a horror gameplay experience where the two streamers' terrified reactions are enjoyable. As the game enters its final phase, the true nature of the monstrous entity, the source of terror, and its psychological invasion are revealed. The player resorts to drastic measures to escape the mental interference and manages to flee, but the lingering aftermath and unsettling ending maintain tension until the very end.

🎨 Mood: A hysterical atmosphere filled with constant screams, terror, and occasional laughter.
