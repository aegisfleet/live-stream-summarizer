📝 Overview

Kiara streamed a sponsored playthrough of 'Sonic Racing: Crossworlds' by SEGA. She challenged herself in this kart racing game featuring characters from the Sonic series, along with guest characters from diverse IPs such as Hatsune Miku, Minecraft, and Yakuza (Like a Dragon). In the viewer-participation online matchmaking, she competed in a total of 9 races, achieving a highest rank of 5th place. In the final race, she engaged in an intense item battle on the new Halloween-themed course, 'Pumpkin Mansion,' and successfully leveled up the game's gadget slot to a Silver Plate. Kiara expressed excitement over the game's quality and fun, concluding the stream by thanking SEGA.
