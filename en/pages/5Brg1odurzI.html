📝 Overview

A collaboration project featuring Hololive members gathering to play 3D Among Us. The 10 participating members were Aki Rosenthal, Sakura Miko, Oozora Subaru, Momosuzu Nene, Himemori Luna, Omaru Polka, Ookami Mio, La+ Darknesss, Tsunomaki Watame, and Yukihana Lamy. The early stages were characterized by frequent blunders and misunderstandings (PON moments), but the coordination among the Imposters shone through in the middle and later stages. Towards the end, the game mode was switched to Infection Mode (Zombie Mode). The Crewmates achieved a task victory, but in the final round, Aki Rosenthal, as the Infected, went on a rampage, quickly achieving a victory by infecting everyone, bringing the chaotic project to a close.
