📝 Overview

Hololive's Shirakami Fubuki played 'Kirby and the Forgotten Land: Starry World'. She continued from her previous clear save data and challenged the new DLC 'Starry World' for the first time. In this stream, she cleared the 'Washed-Up Block Hills' stage, overcoming difficult gimmicks and mid-boss battles. In the latter half, she utilized abilities like Spring Mouthful and Worker Mouthful to tackle underwater puzzle actions and traps, confronting new threats as the story approached its climax. At the end of the stream, she discussed with viewers her hopes for a Kirby movie or anime adaptation, showing enthusiasm for her next stream.
