📝 Overview

Hajime Hiodoshi and Shiranui Flare's collaboration stream 'Yobidashi Bancho'. In this project, the loser becomes a 'subordinate' for a day, and they compete in various Clubhouse Games. Following the first half with 'Blackjack', 'Gomoku Narabe (Connect Five)', 'Yacht', 'Toy Boxing', and 'Air Hockey', the second half features intense battles in 'Bowling' and 'Reversi'. Although Hajime Hiodoshi wins in Bowling, Shiranui Flare achieves a dramatic come-from-behind victory in Reversi, ultimately deciding the outcome of the project. The stream's highlights include their lively conversation and the progression of the games.

🎨 Mood: A stream filled with a consistently friendly atmosphere, lively conversations, and the joy of competition.
