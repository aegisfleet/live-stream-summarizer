📝 Overview

A collaborative stream featuring Hyakki Ayame and Takane Lui for 'The Eminence in Shadow: Master of Garden' (Kage-masu). In the first half of the stream, Hyakki Ayame's in-game implementation, various campaigns, and rerun gachas were introduced. In the latter half, the appeal of the Shadow Festival and the Red Attribute Special Selection Gacha was discussed, and Hyakki Ayame herself attempted the featured gacha. She obtained various SS characters and finally pulled her own SS Hyakki Ayame, expressing immense joy. She set the obtained Hyakki Ayame character to her home screen, increased her Bond Level to enjoy voices and character stories, and finally concluded the stream.

🎨 Mood: Overall bright and lively atmosphere. Hyakki Ayame consistently expressed joy and excitement about her character being implemented in the game. Takane Lui also contributed to the lively stream by interjecting appropriately. The scenes where they fluctuated between joy and disappointment based on gacha results were also memorable.
